Golden Graham S'more

Servings: 1-3
Prep Time : 15-20 minutes
Cooling Time : 30-60 minutes
Total Time : 1 hour 30 minutes

In a large mixing bowl, add the Golden Grahams cereal and set aside. In a medium saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat, then stir in the brown sugar and corn syrup, bringing the mixture to a boil while stirring constantly for 2 minutes.

Remove from heat, and stir in the vanilla extract and salt. Pour the warm syrup over the cereal and gently toss to coat evenly. Fold in the mini marshmallows, being careful not to over-mix so they don’t melt completely.

Transfer the mixture to a greased or parchment-lined 9x9-inch pan, pressing it down firmly to pack it into an even layer.

In a microwave-safe bowl, melt the chocolate chips and butter in 30-second intervals, stirring until smooth, then pour the melted chocolate over the cereal mixture, spreading it evenly.

Let the chocolate set at room temperature for about 10 minutes, then refrigerate for 30-60 minutes to fully harden the chocolate. Once set, cut into squares and serve.

Ingredients List

  • For the Golden Graham S’mores
    • 6 cups Golden Grahams cereal
    • 1 cup mini marshmallows
    • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
    • 1/4 cup packed brown sugar
    • 1/2 cup light corn syrup (or honey as an alternative)
    • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
    • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • For the Chocolate Layer
    • 1 1/2 cups chocolate chips
    • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter (for smoothness and shine)
