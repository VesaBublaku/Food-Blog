Pistachio Loaf

Servings: 1-4
Prep Time : 15-20 minutes
Cook Time : 35-40 minutes
Total Time : 1 hour 10 minutes

Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a 9x5-inch loaf pan or line it with parchment paper. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, and cardamom (if using).

In a large bowl, cream together the softened butter and granulated sugar using an electric mixer until light and fluffy, about 3-5 minutes. Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition, then stir in the vanilla and almond extracts.

Gradually add the dry ingredients to the butter mixture, alternating with the milk, beginning and ending with the dry ingredients, mixing just until combined. Fold in the chopped pistachios until evenly distributed.

Pour the batter into the prepared loaf pan, smoothing the top with a spatula. Bake for 55-65 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

Let the loaf cool in the pan for 10 minutes before transferring it to a wire rack to cool completely. If desired, make a glaze by whisking together powdered sugar, milk, and vanilla extract, and drizzle it over the cooled loaf. Slice, serve, and enjoy!

Ingredients List

  • For the Pistachio Loaf
    • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
    • 1 teaspoon baking powder
    • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
    • 1/4 teaspoon salt
    • 1/2 teaspoon ground cardamom
    • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
    • 1 cup granulated sugar
    • 2 large eggs
    • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
    • 1/2 teaspoon almond extract
    • 1 cup pistachios, shelled and chopped finely
    • 1/2 cup whole milk (or buttermilk for a richer texture)
  • For the Glaze
    • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
    • 1-2 tablespoons milk (or water, to thin)
    • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
