Chorizo Pizza

Servings: 4-5
Prep Time : 20 minutes
Cook Time : 10-12 minutes
Total Time : 35-40 minutes

Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C) and, if using a pizza stone, place it in the oven to heat. On a lightly floured surface, roll out the pizza dough to about 12 inches in diameter and transfer it to a greased pizza pan or parchment-lined pizza peel.

In a small bowl, mix the tomato sauce with minced garlic, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Spread the sauce over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.

Sprinkle half of the mozzarella over the sauce, then top with chorizo slices, red onion, and cherry tomatoes, followed by the remaining mozzarella.

Transfer the pizza to the oven and bake for 10-12 minutes, until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.

After baking, brush the crust lightly with olive oil, then sprinkle torn basil leaves on top along with a pinch of crushed red pepper flakes, fresh arugula, and a sprinkle of Parmesan if desired. Slice and serve warm.

Ingredients List

  • Dough
    • Pizza dough – 1 ball (store-bought or homemade, about 12-14 oz)
    • Olive oil – 1 tablespoon (for brushing the crust)
  • Sauce
    • Tomato sauce – 1/2 cup (preferably a rich, pizza-style sauce)
    • Garlic – 1 clove (minced)
    • Dried oregano – 1/2 teaspoon
    • Salt and black pepper – to taste
  • Toppings
    • Chorizo sausage – 1/2 cup, thinly sliced or crumbled
    • Mozzarella cheese – 1 1/2 cups shredded (for gooey melt)
    • Red onion – 1/4 cup, thinly sliced
    • Cherry tomatoes – 1/2 cup, halved
    • Fresh basil leaves – a handful, torn
  • Optional Finishing Touches
    • Crushed red pepper flakes – for added spice
    • Grated Parmesan cheese – for serving
    • Fresh arugula – a handful (for garnish, optional)
