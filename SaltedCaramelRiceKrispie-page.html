Salted Caramel Rice Krispie

Servings: 1-3
Prep Time : 10-15 minutes
Cooling Time : 30-60 minutes
Total Time : 1 hour

Line a 9x13-inch baking pan with parchment paper or lightly grease it. In a large saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat, then add the mini marshmallows and cook, stirring frequently, until completely melted and smooth, about 3-5 minutes.

Stir in the caramel sauce, vanilla extract, and salt until well combined. Remove the saucepan from heat and gradually fold in the Rice Krispies cereal, stirring gently to coat the cereal without crushing it.

Transfer the mixture to the prepared pan and press it evenly into the pan using a piece of parchment paper or lightly greased hands, making sure it's compact but not too tightly packed.

Let the treats cool at room temperature for 30 minutes to 1 hour, or until firm.

Once cooled, sprinkle with flaky sea salt, cut into squares, and serve. Enjoy!

Ingredients List

  • For the Salted Caramel Rice Krispie
    • 1/4 cup unsalted butter
    • 1 bag (10 oz) mini marshmallows (about 6 cups)
    • 1/2 cup caramel sauce (store-bought or homemade)
    • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
    • 1/4 teaspoon salt (plus extra for sprinkling)
    • 6 cups Rice Krispies cereal
    • Flaky sea salt (for sprinkling on top)
