Creamy Coconut Lime Mojito

Servings: 1
Prep Time : 6 minutes

In a tall glass, muddle the mint leaves with the lime juice and simple syrup to release the mint's aroma and flavor.

Add the coconut cream and rum, then fill the glass with ice and stir well to combine. Top with club soda for a bit of fizz, and gently stir again

Garnish with a lime wheel and a sprig of mint for a refreshing finish.

Ingredients List

  • Base Spirits
    • 2 oz white rum
  • Fruit and Cream

    • 1 oz fresh lime juice
