Basil Smash Piña Colada

Servings: 1
Prep Time : 5 minutes

The Basil Smash Piña Colada is a fresh take on the tropical classic, blending basil's herbal notes with the creamy, fruity flavors of a piña colada.

In a cocktail shaker, gently muddle the basil leaves with the simple syrup (if using) to release the basil's flavor.

Add the rum, coconut cream, pineapple juice, and lime juice to the shaker with ice, and shake well until chilled and creamy. Strain into a glass filled with ice or blend everything with ice for a frozen version.

Garnish with a basil sprig for a fresh finish.

Ingredients List

  • Base Spirits
    • 2 oz white rum
  • Fruit and Cream
    • 1 oz coconut cream
    • 2 oz pineapple juice
    • 1 oz fresh lime juice
  • Herbs
    • 6-8 fresh basil leaves
  • Sweetener
    • ½ oz simple syrup
