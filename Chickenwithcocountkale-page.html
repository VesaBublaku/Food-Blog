Chicken with cocount kale

Servings: 4
Prep Time : 10 minutes
Cook Time : 25 minutes
Total Time : 35 minutes

Season 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts with salt, pepper, and 1 teaspoon paprika.

Heat 1 tablespoon olive or coconut oil in a skillet over medium heat, then add the chicken and cook for 6-8 minutes on each side, or until golden brown and fully cooked through. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside, keeping it covered.

In the same skillet, add 1 tablespoon coconut oil, followed by 1 small finely chopped onion, and sauté until softened, about 3 minutes. Add 3 minced garlic cloves, cooking for an additional minute, then pour in a 14 oz can of coconut milk and bring it to a gentle simmer. Stir in 4 cups chopped kale, 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional), salt, and pepper to taste, cover, and cook for about 5 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the kale is tender and infused with the coconut milk.

Finish with a squeeze of juice from half a lime. To serve, plate the chicken with a generous side of coconut kale, spooning any remaining coconut sauce over the top for extra flavor.

Ingredients List

  • For the Chicken
    • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
    • Salt and pepper, to taste
    • 1 teaspoon paprika
    • 1 tablespoon olive oil or coconut oil
  • For the Coconut Kale
    • 1 tablespoon coconut oil
    • 1 small onion, finely chopped
    • 3 cloves garlic, minced
    • 1 can (14 oz) coconut milk
    • 1 bunch kale, stems removed and chopped
    • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes
    • Juice of 1/2 lime
