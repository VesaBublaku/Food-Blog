Chai Espresso Martini

Servings: 1
Prep Time : 5 minutes

For a Chai Espresso Martini, start by brewing a strong shot of espresso and letting it cool slightly

Fill a cocktail shaker with ice and add 1 oz of vodka, 1 oz of coffee liqueur, 1 oz of the cooled espresso, and 1 oz of chai concentrate (or cooled chai tea if you prefer a lighter flavor).

Shake well until the mixture is chilled and frothy. Strain into a chilled martini glass, then garnish with a sprinkle of ground cinnamon or a few coffee beans for an aromatic finish.

Ingredients List

  • Alcohol
    • 1 oz vodka
    • 1 oz (30 ml) coffee liqueur
  • Coffee
    • 1 oz espresso
  • Tea
    • 1 oz chai concentrate
  • Garnish
    • Coffee beans
    • Cinnamon stick
