Ginger Chicken Meatball Sandos

Servings: 4 sandwiches
Prep Time : 15 minutes
Cook Time : 15 - 20 minutes
Total Time : 30 - 35 minutes

To make Ginger Chicken Meatball Sandos, begin by preparing the meatball mixture in a large bowl, combining ground chicken, breadcrumbs, an egg, soy sauce, freshly grated ginger, minced garlic, chopped green onions, salt, and pepper.

Mix thoroughly until everything is evenly incorporated, then shape the mixture into small, evenly sized meatballs, aiming for about 1 to 1.5 inches in diameter; this should yield around 12-14 meatballs. Heat a tablespoon of sesame oil in a skillet over medium heat, adding the meatballs in a single layer.

Allow them to cook for 10-12 minutes, turning them occasionally to ensure all sides brown evenly, until theyâ€™re golden and fully cooked through.

Meanwhile, prepare the spicy mayo sauce by whisking together mayonnaise, sriracha, soy sauce, and rice vinegar in a small bowl until smooth, adjusting the level of sriracha to control the spice level to your preference. Once the meatballs are done, begin assembling the sandwiches: slice the buns or brioche rolls in half and spread a generous layer of the sriracha mayo on the bottom half of each bun.

Arrange 3-4 meatballs on each bun, then top with a handful of shredded cabbage for crunch, a few cucumber slices for freshness, and sprigs of fresh cilantro for an herbaceous flavor. Place the top bun on each sandwich and press down lightly. Serve each sando with a lime wedge on the side for an optional citrusy kick that enhances the flavors.

These Ginger Chicken Meatball Sandos are perfect for a satisfying lunch or dinner, balancing savory, spicy, and fresh notes in each bite.

Ingredients List

  • For the Meatballs
    • 1 lb ground chicken
    • 1/4 cup breadcrumbs
    • 1 egg
    • 1 tbsp soy sauce
    • 1 tbsp grated fresh ginger
    • 2 cloves garlic, minced
    • 2 green onions
    • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • For the Sauce
    • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
    • 1 tbsp sriracha
    • 1 tsp soy sauce
    • 1/2 tsp rice vinegar
  • For Assembly
    • 4 soft sandwich rolls or brioche buns
    • 1 cup shredded cabbage
    • 1/2 cucumber
    • Fresh cilantro leaves
    • Lime wedges
