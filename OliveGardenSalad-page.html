Olive Garden Salad

Servings: 4 - 6 servings
Prep Time : 10 minutes
Total Time : 10 minutes

To make Olive Garden-style Salad, start by chopping 1 head of Romaine lettuce and placing it in a large bowl.

Add 1/2 thinly sliced red onion, 1 sliced cucumber, 1 cup halved cherry tomatoes, 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese, 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese, 1/4 cup sliced black olives, and 1/4 cup pepperoncini peppers (if desired).

In a separate bowl, whisk together 1/4 cup red wine vinegar, 1/2 cup olive oil, 1 tsp Dijon mustard, 1 clove minced garlic, 1/2 tsp sugar, salt and pepper to taste, 1/2 tsp dried oregano, 1/2 tsp dried basil, and 1/4 tsp onion powder until well combined.

Pour the dressing over the salad, toss to coat, and top with 1/2 cup croutons before serving. Enjoy the fresh, tangy flavors of your homemade salad!

Ingredients List

  • For the Chicken
    • 1 head of Romaine lettuce, chopped
    • 1/2 small red onion, thinly sliced
    • 1 cucumber, sliced
    • 1 cup cherry tomatoes
    • 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
    • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
    • 1/2 cup croutons
    • 1/4 cup black olives
    • 1/4 cup pepperoncini
  • For the Dreesing
    • 1/4 cup red wine vinegar
    • 1/2 cup olive oil
    • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
    • 11 clove garlic, minced
    • 1/2 tsp sugar
    • Salt and pepper to taste
    • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
    • 1/2 tsp dried basil
    • 1/4 tsp onion powder
