Vegetarian Shepherd’s Pie

Servings: 6 - 8 servings
Prep Time : 15 minutes
Cook Time : 35 - 40 minutes
Total Time : 50 - 55 minutes

For a hearty vegetarian shepherd’s pie, start by preheating your oven to 400°F (200°C) and greasing a 9-inch baking dish.

For the filling, heat 2 tablespoons of olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add 1 chopped onion, 2 cloves of minced garlic, 2 medium carrots (diced), and 1 cup of diced celery, and sauté until softened, about 5-7 minutes.

Stir in 1 cup of peas and 1/2 cup of corn (optional) and cook for another 2 minutes. Add 2 cups of cooked lentils (or canned lentils, drained and rinsed) and 1 tablespoon of tomato paste. Stir in 1 1/2 cups vegetable broth, 1 tablespoon soy sauce, 1 teaspoon dried thyme, 1 teaspoon dried rosemary, salt, and pepper to taste. Let the mixture simmer for about 5-7 minutes until thickened.

Transfer the filling to the prepared baking dish.For the mashed potatoes, peel and boil 4 large potatoes in salted water until tender, about 15 minutes.

Drain, then mash with 1/4 cup butter, 1/4 cup milk, and salt and pepper to taste until smooth and creamy. Spread the mashed potatoes evenly over the vegetable filling, smoothing the top with a spatula.

Bake the shepherd’s pie for 20 minutes, or until the top is golden and slightly crispy. For extra crispiness, broil for an additional 2-3 minutes. Let it rest for a few minutes before serving.

Ingredients List

  • For the filling
    • 2 tablespoons olive oil
    • 1 onion, chopped
    • 2 cloves garlic, minced
    • 2 medium carrots, diced
    • 1 cup celery, diced
    • 1 cup peas
    • 1/2 cup corn (optional)
    • 2 cups cooked lentils
    • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
    • 1 1/2 cups vegetable broth
    • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
    • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
    • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
    • Salt and pepper
  • For the mashed potatoes
    • 4 large potatoes, peeled and chopped
    • 1/4 cup butter
    • 1/4 cup milk
    • Salt and pepper
