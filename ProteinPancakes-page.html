Protein Pancakes

Servings: 2-4
Prep Time : 5-7 minutes
Cook Time : 2-3 minutes
Total Time : 15-20 minutes

In a large bowl, combine the oats (or oat flour), baking powder, cinnamon, salt, and protein powder, then set aside. In a separate bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, vanilla extract, and Greek yogurt until smooth, and mash the banana if using, then stir it into the wet mixture for added sweetness and moisture.

Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and mix until just combined, adding more milk if needed to reach a thick but pourable consistency.

If desired, fold in chia seeds, blueberries, or chocolate chips. Heat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat, adding a small amount of coconut oil or butter.

Pour 1/4 cup of batter per pancake onto the skillet and cook for 2-3 minutes, until bubbles form on the surface and edges set, then flip and cook for another 1-2 minutes until golden brown.

Serve with your favorite toppings, such as fresh fruit, nut butter, honey, or maple syrup.

Ingredients List

  • Dry Ingredients
    • Oats (rolled oats or oat flour) – 1 cup
    • Baking powder – 2 teaspoons
    • Ground cinnamon – 1/2 teaspoon
    • Salt – 1/4 teaspoon
    • Protein powder – 1/2 cup (preferably unflavored)
  • Wet Ingredients
    • Eggs – 2 large
    • Milk – 3/4 cup (any milk of choice, almond, oat, or regular milk)
    • Vanilla extract – 1 teaspoon
    • Greek yogurt – 1/4 cup (for extra creaminess and protein)
  • Optional Add-ins
    • Banana – 1/2 (mashed, for natural sweetness)
    • Chia seeds – 1 tablespoon (for added texture and nutrition)
    • Blueberries or chocolate chips – 1/4 cup (optional)
