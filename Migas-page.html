Migas

Servings: 3-4
Prep Time : 5-10 minutes
Cook Time : 15 minutes
Total Time : 20-25 minutes

Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium heat, then add the tortilla strips and fry, stirring occasionally, until golden and crispy, about 3-4 minutes. Remove the strips from the skillet and set them aside on a paper towel-lined plate to drain excess oil. In the same skillet, remove any excess oil, leaving just enough to sauté the vegetables.

Add the onion, bell pepper, and jalapeño, and cook for 4-5 minutes until they soften. Add the garlic and cook for another minute until fragrant, then stir in the diced tomato and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes until softened.

In a bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, salt, and black pepper, then pour the egg mixture into the skillet with the vegetables.

Let the eggs cook for a few seconds, then gently stir to scramble, cooking until they are just set. Add the tortilla strips and cheese, folding them into the eggs until the cheese melts.

Remove from heat, then sprinkle with cilantro and green onions. Serve the migas with sliced avocado, salsa, and hot sauce if desired. Enjoy!

Ingredients List

  • Tortilla Strips
    • 4 small corn tortillas, cut into 1-inch strips
    • 2-3 tbsp vegetable oil (for frying)
  • Vegetables and Aromatics
    • 1/2 cup onion, finely chopped
    • 1/2 cup bell pepper (any color), finely chopped
    • 1 jalapeño, seeded and finely chopped (optional, for extra heat)
    • 2 cloves garlic, minced
    • 1 medium tomato, diced
  • Eggs and Dairy
    • 6 large eggs
    • 1/4 cup milk (optional, for fluffier eggs)
    • 1/2 cup shredded cheese (such as cheddar, Monterey Jack)
  • Seasonings
    • Salt and black pepper, to taste
    • 1/4 tsp chili powder (optional, for a touch of smokiness)
  • Toppings
    • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
    • 1/4 cup green onions, chopped
    • 1 avocado, sliced
    • Salsa (optional, for serving)
    • Hot sauce (optional, for serving)
