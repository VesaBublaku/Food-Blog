Spicy Sausage Pasta

Servings: 4 servings
Prep Time : 10 minutes
Cook Time : 20 minutes
Total Time : 30 minutes

To make Spicy Sausage Pasta, heat 1 tbsp olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat, then add the spicy Italian sausage.

Cook, breaking it apart, until browned, about 5-7 minutes, then remove and set aside. In the same skillet, add the diced onion and cook for 3-4 minutes until softened, then add the minced garlic and diced red bell pepper, cooking for another 2 minutes. Stir in 1 tsp red pepper flakes, 1 can of diced tomatoes, 1 cup heavy cream, and 1/2 cup chicken broth.

Bring the sauce to a simmer and season with salt and pepper to taste. Meanwhile, cook the pasta according to package instructions, then drain.

Add the cooked pasta and browned sausage back into the skillet, stirring well to coat in the sauce. Stir in 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese until melted and combined. Garnish with fresh parsley or basil and serve hot.

  • For the Pasta
    • 1 lb spicy Italian sausage, casing removed
    • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
    • 1 small onion, diced
    • 3 garlic cloves, minced
    • 1 red bell pepper, diced
    • 1 tsp red pepper flakes
    • 1 (14.5 oz) can diced tomatoes
    • 1 cup heavy cream
    • 1/2 cup chicken broth
    • 10 oz pasta
    • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
    • Salt and pepper to taste
    • Fresh parsley or basil
