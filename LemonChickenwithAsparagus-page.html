Lemon Chicken with Asparagus

Servings: 4 servings
Prep Time : 10-15 minutes
Cook Time : 20-25 minutes
Total Time : 30-40 minutes

Pat the chicken breasts dry with paper towels and season both sides with salt, pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, thyme, and oregano. Heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat and sear the chicken for 6–7 minutes on the first side, then flip and cook for another 5–6 minutes, or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C).

Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside. In the same skillet, add 1 tablespoon of olive oil and sauté the asparagus for 4–5 minutes, stirring occasionally, until tender-crisp, then season with salt and pepper and set aside.

In the same skillet, add the minced garlic and cook for 30 seconds until fragrant, then add the lemon zest, lemon juice, and chicken broth (or white wine), scraping up any browned bits from the pan. Let the sauce simmer for 2–3 minutes, allowing it to reduce slightly.

Return the chicken to the skillet, spoon some of the sauce over it, and simmer for another 2–3 minutes. Add the asparagus back into the skillet, allowing it to soak up some of the sauce.

Serve the chicken with the asparagus, spoon the lemon sauce over the top, and garnish with fresh parsley or thyme, adding lemon wedges on the side for extra flavor.

Ingredients List

  • For the Chicken
    • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts (about 6 ounces each)
    • 2 tablespoons olive oil (divided)
    • Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste
    • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
    • 1 teaspoon onion powder
    • 1 teaspoon dried thyme (or fresh thyme, if preferred)
    • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
    • Zest and juice of 1 lemon
    • 1/2 cup low-sodium chicken broth or white wine (for deglazing)
    • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • FFor the Asparagus
    • 1 bunch of asparagus, trimmed (about 1 pound)
    • 1 tablespoon olive oil
    • Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste
    • 1 tablespoon lemon juice (optional, for extra brightness)
  • Garnishes (optional)
    • Fresh parsley or thyme, chopped
    • Lemon wedges
