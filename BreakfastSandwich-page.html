Breakfast Sandwich

Servings: 1
Prep Time : 5 minutes
Cook Time : 10 minutes
Total Time : 15 minutes

Start by buttering one side of each slice of bread, then heat a skillet over medium heat and toast the bread, buttered side down, until golden brown and crispy, about 2-3 minutes per side, then set aside.

In the same skillet, crack the egg and cook it to your desired doneness, seasoning with salt and pepper (2-3 minutes for sunny-side-up, or 4-5 minutes for a firmer yolk).

While the egg cooks, heat another skillet and cook the bacon or breakfast meat until crispy, about 3-5 minutes (or until fully cooked if using sausage).

Once the bread is toasted, place a slice of cheese on one piece of bread so it melts slightly, then layer the cooked egg and bacon (or other meat) on top.

Add optional toppings like avocado, tomato, or spinach, then place the second slice of toasted bread on top to complete the sandwich. Slice in half and serve immediately.

Ingredients List

  • For the Sandwich
    • 2 slices of bread (English muffin, sourdough, whole grain)
    • 1 large egg
    • 2 slices of cooked bacon (or sausage patty, ham)
    • 1 slice of cheese (cheddar, American, Swiss, or your favorite cheese)
    • Butter (for toasting the bread)
    • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Optional Add-ins
    • Sliced avocado (for creaminess)
    • Tomato slices (for freshness)
    • Fresh spinach or arugula (for greens)
    • Hot sauce or mustard (for some extra flavor kick)
