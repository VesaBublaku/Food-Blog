Spicy Pumpkin Ginger Soup

Servings: 5 servings
Prep Time : 10-15 minutes
Cook Time : 20-25 minutes
Total Time : 45-55 minutes

Heat olive oil (or butter) in a large pot or Dutch oven over medium heat, then add the chopped onion and sauté for about 5 minutes, stirring occasionally until the onion becomes soft and translucent. Add the minced garlic, diced carrot, celery, and potato to the pot, and cook for another 5-7 minutes, stirring frequently, until the vegetables are softened.

Once the vegetables are tender, stir in the ground cumin, ground coriander, ground ginger, cayenne pepper, smoked paprika (if using), and ground cinnamon, and cook for about 1 minute to allow the spices to become fragrant and toasted. Then, pour in the pumpkin puree and vegetable broth, stirring everything together until the mixture is well combined. Bring the soup to a simmer over medium-high heat, then reduce the heat to medium-low, allowing the soup to cook uncovered for about 20-25 minutes, or until the potatoes and carrots are fully tender and cooked through.

Once the vegetables are soft, use an immersion blender to blend the soup until it is smooth and creamy. If you don’t have an immersion blender, you can carefully transfer the soup to a blender in batches and blend until smooth before returning it to the pot. If the soup is too thick, you can add more broth or water to reach your desired consistency.

Stir in the coconut milk (or heavy cream) and cook for an additional 5-7 minutes to allow the soup to become creamy and well-mixed. Taste the soup and adjust the seasoning with salt and black pepper as needed. For an extra burst of brightness, add a tablespoon of fresh lime juice and stir it in.

Once the soup is ready, ladle it into bowls and garnish with a dollop of sour cream or Greek yogurt, a sprinkle of chopped cilantro, and a handful of toasted pumpkin seeds. Serve with crusty bread for dipping and enjoy this warming, spicy pumpkin soup!

Ingredients List

  • For the Soup
    • 2 tablespoons olive oil (or butter)
    • 1 medium yellow onion, chopped
    • 2 cloves garlic, minced
    • 1 medium carrot, peeled and diced
    • 1 celery stalk, diced
    • 1 medium potato, peeled and diced (for added creaminess)
    • 4 cups pumpkin puree (fresh or canned)
    • 4 cups vegetable broth (or chicken broth)
    • 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
    • 1/2 teaspoon ground coriander
    • 1 teaspoon ground ginger
    • 1/2 to 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper (adjust to your spice preference)
    • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika (optional, for a smoky depth)
    • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
    • Salt and black pepper to taste
    • 1/2 cup coconut milk (or heavy cream for a richer soup)
    • 1 tablespoon fresh lime juice (optional, for brightness)
  • For Garnishing (optional)
    • Sour cream or Greek yogurt
    • Chopped fresh cilantro
    • Pumpkin seeds (pepitas), toasted
    • Crusty bread for dipping
