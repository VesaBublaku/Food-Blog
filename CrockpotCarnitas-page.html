Crockpot Carnitas

Servings: 6 - 8 servings
Prep Time : 15 minutes
Cook Time : 3 hours
Total Time : 8 - 10 hours(low) or 4 - 6 hours(high)

To make Crockpot Carnitas, start by seasoning 3-4 lbs of pork shoulder with salt and pepper. In a large skillet, heat 1 tbsp olive oil over medium-high heat and sear the pork on all sides until golden brown, about 2-3 minutes per side.

Transfer the pork to the crockpot, then add 1 cup orange juice, 1/4 cup lime juice, 4 minced garlic cloves, 1 quartered onion, and sliced jalapeño (if using). In a small bowl, combine 1 tbsp each of chili powder, ground cumin, and oregano, along with 1 tsp smoked paprika; sprinkle this mixture over the pork in the crockpot.

Cover and cook on low for 8-10 hours, or on high for 4-6 hours, until the pork is tender and easily shreds with a fork. Once cooked, remove the pork from the crockpot, shred it, and spread it on a baking sheet.

Broil for 3-5 minutes until crispy, if desired. Serve the carnitas in warm tortillas with fresh cilantro and your favorite toppings

Ingredients List

  • For the Cranitas
    • 3-4 lbs pork shoulder
    • 1 tbsp olive oil
    • 1 cup orange juice
    • 1/4 cup lime juice
    • 4 garlic cloves, minced
    • 1 onion, quartered
    • 1 jalapeño, sliced
    • 1 tbsp chili powder
    • 1 tbsp ground cumin
    • 1 tbsp oregano
    • 1 tsp smoked paprika
    • Salt and pepper to taste
    • Fresh cilantro, chopped
    • Corn or flour tortillas
