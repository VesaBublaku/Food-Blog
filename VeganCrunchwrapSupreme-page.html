Vegan Crunchwrap Supreme

Servings: 4
Prep Time : 15 minutes
Cook Time : 15 - 20 minutes
Total Time : 30 - 35 minutes

To make a Vegan Crunchwrap Supreme, start by heating olive oil in a skillet and cooking your vegan protein (tempeh, soy-based meat, or lentils) for 5-7 minutes, then stir in refried beans, cumin, chili powder, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper, and cook for another 3-5 minutes.

Warm the flour tortillas in a skillet until pliable, then lay one flat and add a layer of the bean-protein mixture in the center. Top with shredded vegan cheese, guacamole, sour cream, shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, and taco sauce.

Place a second tortilla on top, fold the edges of the larger tortilla over the filling to seal, and press gently. Heat a skillet over medium heat and cook the wrapped tortilla seam-side down for 2-3 minutes on each side until golden and crispy. Repeat with the remaining wraps and serve with extra salsa or hot sauce.

Ingredients List

  • For the Filling
    • 1 tbsp olive oil
    • 1 can (15 oz) refried beans
    • 1 tsp ground cumin
    • 1 tsp chili powder
    • 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
    • 1 cup vegan protein
    • Salt and pepper, to taste
    • 1/2 cup cooked rice
    • 1/2 cup salsa
  • Additional Layers
    • 4 large flour tortillas
    • 1 cup vegan cheese
    • 1/2 cup guacamole
    • 1/2 cup vegan sour cream
    • Lettuce, shredded
    • Tomato, diced
    • Taco sauce or your favorite hot sauce
