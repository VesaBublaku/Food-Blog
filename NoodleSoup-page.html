Noodle Soup

Servings: 4
Prep Time : 15 minutes
Cook Time : 30 minutes
Total Time : 45 minutes

To make this hearty noodle soup, start by preparing your protein if you’re using any. For chicken, slice one breast thinly, season with salt and pepper, and cook in a skillet until fully done

For tofu, cube it, and for shrimp, rinse and remove tails if desired. Set the cooked protein aside

Next, in a large pot, heat sesame oil over medium heat, then add sliced ginger, minced garlic, and sliced onion, sautéing for 2-3 minutes until fragrant. Add the star anise and cinnamon stick (optional) and stir for another minute. Pour in the chicken or vegetable broth along with soy sauce and fish sauce, bringing the mixture to a boil, then reducing the heat to let it simmer for 10-15 minutes to allow the flavors to meld

After simmering, remove the star anise and cinnamon stick. Add julienned carrot, sliced mushrooms, red bell pepper, and zucchini to the pot, simmering for about 5 more minutes until the vegetables are tender

While the broth simmers, cook your noodles according to package instructions in a separate pot, then drain and set them aside. Taste the broth and adjust the seasoning if needed with extra soy sauce or salt. Add spinach or bok choy leaves just before serving to let them wilt slightly.

To assemble, divide the noodles among serving bowls, then ladle the hot broth and vegetables over the noodles, topping with the prepared protein if using. Garnish with green onions, fresh cilantro, bean sprouts, and sliced chili peppers if desired, and serve with lime wedges, chili oil, or extra soy sauce on the side.

Ingredients List

  • Broth
    • 8 cups low-sodium chicken
    • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
    • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
    • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
    • 1-inch ginger,sliced
    • 3 garlic cloves
    • 1 small onion,sliced
    • 1 star anise pod(optional)
  • Vegetables
    • 1 large carrot
    • 1 cup mushrooms
    • 1 red bell pepper
    • 1 small zucchini
    • 1-2 green onions
    • Fresh spinach or bok choy leaves
  • Noodles
    • 8 oz noodles
  • Toopings
    • Fresh cilantro
    • Bean sprouts
    • Lime wedges
    • Extra soy sauce or chili oil
