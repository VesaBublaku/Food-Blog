Salmon with Avocado salsa

Servings: 4
Prep Time : 10 minutes
Cook Time : 15 minutes
Total Time : 25 minutes

Preheat the grill, stovetop grill pan, or oven (400°F for baking).

Pat 4 salmon fillets dry and season with salt, pepper, smoked paprika, and garlic powder, then brush with olive oil. Grill, pan-sear, or bake salmon until it flakes easily and reaches an internal temperature of 145°F (about 5-6 minutes per side for grilling, 4-5 minutes per side for pan-searing, or 12-15 minutes for baking).

While the salmon cooks, prepare the salsa by combining 2 diced avocados, 1 cup quartered cherry tomatoes, 1/4 cup diced red onion, 1/4 cup chopped cilantro, and 1 finely chopped jalapeño (optional) with juice from 1 lime, and season with salt and pepper.

Top each salmon fillet with a generous spoonful of avocado salsa and serve with lemon wedges.

Ingredients List

  • For the Salmon
    • 4 salmon fillets
    • 1 tablespoon olive oil
    • Salt and pepper
    • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
    • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
    • Lemon wedges
  • For the Avocado Salsa
    • 2 ripe avocados, diced
    • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, quartered
    • 1/4 cup red onion, finely diced
    • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro
    • 1 jalapeño, seeded and finely chopped
    • Juice of 1 lime
    • Salt and pepper, to taste
