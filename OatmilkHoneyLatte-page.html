Oatmilk Honey Latte

Servings: 1
Prep Time : 1-2 minutes
Cook Time : 4-5 minutes
Total Time : 5-7 minutes

Brew one shot of espresso (or 1/2 cup of strong brewed coffee if you don’t have an espresso machine) and set it aside. While the espresso brews, pour 1 cup of oat milk into a small saucepan and heat it over medium heat until warm but not boiling (about 160°F or 70°C).

Froth the oat milk using a milk frother, immersion blender, or by whisking vigorously to create a creamy, foamy texture.

Once the oat milk is heated and frothed, stir in 1-2 tablespoons of honey (adjust to your sweetness preference) and 1/2 teaspoon of vanilla extract (if using), then mix until the honey dissolves completely.

Pour the brewed espresso into a mug, then slowly pour the frothed oat milk over the espresso, holding back the foam with a spoon, and once the liquid is poured, spoon the foam on top.

Optionally, garnish with a sprinkle of ground cinnamon, nutmeg, or cocoa powder, and serve immediately.

Ingredients List

  • For the Latte
    • 1 shot of espresso (or 1/2 cup strong brewed coffee)
    • 1 cup oat milk (any variety, but barista oat milk froths the best)
    • 1-2 tbsp honey (depending on your desired sweetness)
    • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract (optional, for extra flavor)
  • Optional Garnishes
    • Ground cinnamon or nutmeg, for sprinkling
    • Cocoa powder or chocolate shavings (optional, for a chocolatey twist)
