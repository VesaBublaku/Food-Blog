Roasted Cauliflower Bowl

Servings: 2
Prep Time : 10 minutes
Cook Time : 25 - 30 minutes
Total Time : 35 - 40 minutes

To prepare a Roasted Cauliflower Bowl, start by preheating your oven to 400°F (200°C).

Cut a medium head of cauliflower into florets and toss them in a mixing bowl with olive oil, ground turmeric, cumin, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper, ensuring the cauliflower is well coated.

Spread the seasoned florets evenly on a baking sheet and roast for 25-30 minutes, flipping halfway through, until the cauliflower is golden and tender. While the cauliflower is roasting, cook your choice of grain such as quinoa, rice, or couscous according to package instructions.

Once the cauliflower is roasted and the grains are ready, begin assembling the bowl by layering a base of your cooked grain, followed by a generous amount of leafy greens like spinach, arugula, or kale. Add the roasted cauliflower on top, along with slices of creamy avocado and optional roasted chickpeas for added crunch.

Drizzle with tahini dressing or any dressing of your choice, and finish by garnishing with fresh herbs like cilantro, parsley, or mint. Serve with a lemon wedge on the side for a burst of freshness. This wholesome and customizable bowl makes a perfect nourishing meal!

Ingredients List

  • For the Roasted Cauliflower
    • 1 medium head of cauliflower, cut into florets
    • 2 tbsp olive oil
    • 1 tsp ground turmeric
    • 1 tsp cumin
    • ½ tsp smoked paprika
    • Salt and pepper
  • For the Bowl
    • 1 cup cooked quinoa
    • 1 cup leafy greens
    • ¼ cup roasted chickpeas
    • ½ avocado, sliced
    • ¼ cup tahini dressing
    • Fresh herbs
    • Lemon wedges, for serving
