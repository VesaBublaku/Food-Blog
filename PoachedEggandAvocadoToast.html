Poached Egg and Avocado Toast

Servings: 1-3
Prep Time : 5 minutes
Cook Time : 10-15 minutes
Total Time : 15-20 minutes

Fill a medium saucepan with water, add 1-2 tbsp vinegar (optional), and bring it to a gentle simmer over medium heat. Crack each egg into a small cup and gently slide them into the simmering water, cooking for 3-4 minutes for runny yolks or 5-6 minutes for firmer yolks, then carefully remove with a slotted spoon and set on paper towels.

While the eggs are poaching, heat a skillet over medium heat, melt 1 tbsp butter (or add olive oil), and toast the bread for 2-3 minutes on each side until golden and crispy.

Meanwhile, mash the avocado in a bowl with a fork, adding lemon juice, salt, pepper, and chili flakes (if using), and stir in olive oil for extra creaminess.

Once the bread is toasted, spread the mashed avocado evenly on each slice. Place a poached egg on top of each avocado toast and garnish with optional toppings like tomato slices, feta cheese, fresh herbs, or microgreens.

Serve immediately and enjoy!

Ingredients List

  • For the Poached Eggs
    • 2 large eggs (or more depending on servings)
    • 1-2 tbsp vinegar (optional, helps eggs hold together)
    • Water (for poaching)
  • For the Toast
    • 2 slices of your favorite bread (sourdough, whole grain)
    • 1 tbsp butter or olive oil (for toasting)
  • For the Avocado Mash
    • 1 ripe avocado
    • 1 tbsp lemon juice (or lime juice)
    • Salt and black pepper, to taste
    • 1/4 tsp chili flakes (optional, for some heat)
    • 1 tbsp olive oil (optional, for extra creaminess)
    • Fresh herbs (such as parsley or cilantro), for garnish (optional)
  • Optional Toppings
    • Tomato slices (optional)
    • Feta cheese, crumbled (optional)
    • Microgreens or arugula (optional, for added freshness)
