Pumpkin Caramel Bread

Servings: 1-4
Prep Time : 15-20 minutes
Cook Time : 60-70 minutes
Total Time : 1 hour 30 minutes

Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a 9x5-inch loaf pan. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, baking powder, cinnamon, nutmeg, ginger, and salt. In a large bowl, cream the softened butter and granulated sugar until light and fluffy.

Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition, then stir in the pumpkin puree and vanilla extract. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing just until combined, then fold in the buttermilk.

If desired, add chopped pecans or walnuts. Pour the batter into the prepared pan and smooth the top. Bake for 60-70 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean or with a few moist crumbs.

Let the bread cool in the pan for 10 minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely. Meanwhile, make the caramel sauce by melting the butter in a saucepan, stirring in the brown sugar, and bringing it to a simmer for 3-4 minutes.

Stir in the heavy cream, vanilla extract, and a pinch of salt. Let the sauce cool slightly, then drizzle it over the cooled pumpkin bread. Slice and enjoy!

Ingredients List

  • For the Pumpkin Bread
    • 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
    • 1 teaspoon baking soda
    • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
    • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
    • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
    • 1/4 teaspoon ground ginger
    • 1/4 teaspoon salt
    • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
    • 1 cup granulated sugar
    • 2 large eggs
    • 1 can (15 oz) pumpkin puree
    • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
    • 1/2 cup buttermilk
    • 1/2 cup chopped pecans or walnuts (optional)
  • For the Caramel Sauce
    • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
    • 1 cup packed brown sugar (light or dark)
    • 1/4 cup heavy cream
    • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
    • Pinch of salt
