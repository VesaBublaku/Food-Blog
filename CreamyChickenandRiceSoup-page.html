Creamy Chicken and Rice Soup

Servings: 4 - 6 servings
Prep Time : 10 minutes
Cook Time : 25 - 30 minutes
Total Time : 35 - 40 minutes

Heat 2 tbsp olive oil in a large pot over medium heat, then saut√© 1 diced onion, 2 diced carrots, and 2 diced celery stalks for 5-6 minutes until softened. Add 3 minced garlic cloves and cook for 1 more minute.

Pour in 4 cups chicken broth and add 2 cups of shredded or diced cooked chicken, bringing the mixture to a simmer for 5-7 minutes.

Stir in 1 cup of cooked rice, 1/2 tsp dried thyme, 1/2 tsp dried parsley, and salt and pepper to taste, simmering for another 5 minutes. Reduce the heat to low and stir in 1 cup heavy cream and 1/2 cup milk, cooking for 5-10 minutes until creamy and heated through.

Adjust the seasoning with salt, pepper, and 1 tbsp lemon juice if desired, then serve hot.

Ingredients List

  • Vegetables
    • 1 medium onion, diced
    • 2 carrots, peeled and diced
    • 2 celery stalks, diced
    • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • Liquids
    • 4 cups chicken broth
    • 1 cup heavy cream
    • 1/2 cup milk
  • Protein and Grains
    • 2 cups cooked chicken
    • 1 cup cooked rice
