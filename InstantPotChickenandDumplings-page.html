Instant Pot Chicken and Dumplings

Servings: 2 servings
Prep Time : 15 minutes
Cook Time : 25 minutes
Total Time : 40 minutes

To make Instant Pot Chicken and Dumplings, first set your Instant Pot to the "Sauté" function and heat 2 tablespoons of olive oil. Add the chopped onion, carrots, and celery, sautéing for 3-4 minutes until softened. Add the minced garlic and cook for an additional 30 seconds.

Next, add the chicken breasts (or thighs), dried thyme, rosemary, turmeric (if using), bay leaf, salt, and pepper, then pour in the chicken broth and half-and-half (or cream), ensuring the chicken is submerged. Close the lid, set the valve to "Sealing," and cook on the "Pressure Cook" or "Manual" setting for 10 minutes (12 minutes if using frozen chicken).

Once done, let the pressure release naturally for 5 minutes before quick-releasing the remaining pressure. Remove the chicken and shred it using two forks, then return the shredded chicken to the pot along with the frozen peas, stirring to combine and adjusting the seasoning with more salt and pepper if needed.

In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, salt, garlic powder, and dried parsley, then add the milk, melted butter, and egg to form a thick, slightly sticky batter. Spoon the dumpling batter over the soup, spacing them out, then close the lid again, set the valve to "Sealing," and cook on "Pressure Cook" for 5 minutes.

Let the pressure release naturally for 5 minutes before quick-releasing the remaining pressure. Stir gently, then ladle the chicken and dumplings into bowls and serve hot, garnished with fresh parsley if desired.

Ingredients List

  • For the Chicken and Soup Base
    • 1 lb (about 2-3) boneless, skinless chicken breasts or thighs
    • 1 medium onion, chopped
    • 2 cloves garlic, minced
    • 3 medium carrots, peeled and sliced
    • 2 celery stalks, chopped
    • 4 cups chicken broth (low-sodium recommended)
    • 1 cup half-and-half or heavy cream
    • 1 cup frozen peas
    • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
    • 1/2 teaspoon dried rosemary
    • 1/2 teaspoon ground turmeric
    • Salt and pepper to taste
    • 2 tablespoons olive oil
    • 1 bay leaf
  • For the Dumplings
    • 1 cup all-purpose flour
    • 1 tablespoon baking powder
    • 1/2 teaspoon salt
    • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder (optional)
    • 1/2 teaspoon dried parsley (optional)
    • 1/2 cup milk
    • 3 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
    • 1 large egg
