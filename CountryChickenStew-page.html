Country Chicken Stew

Servings: 4- 9 servings
Prep Time : 10 minutes
Cook Time : 55-60 minutes
Total Time : 1 hour 10 minutes

In a large pot or Dutch oven, heat 2 tablespoons of olive oil over medium-high heat. Season 1 1/2 pounds of chicken thighs generously with salt and pepper, then add them to the pot and sear for about 4-5 minutes per side until golden brown; remove and set aside.

In the same pot, add 1 chopped onion, 2 diced carrots, and 2 diced celery stalks, and saut√© for about 5 minutes until softened. Add 3 minced garlic cloves and cook for another minute until fragrant. Sprinkle 2 tablespoons of flour over the vegetables, stir to coat, and cook for 1 minute to remove the raw flour taste.

Slowly pour in 6 cups of chicken broth, stirring to combine and deglaze the pot, scraping up any browned bits from the bottom. Add 2 peeled and diced potatoes, 1 bay leaf, 1 teaspoon dried thyme, and 1 teaspoon dried rosemary. Return the chicken to the pot, bring the stew to a simmer, and then reduce the heat to low.

Cover and cook for 35-40 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the chicken is tender and the vegetables are cooked through. After cooking, remove the chicken, shred it with two forks, and discard any bones.

Return the shredded chicken to the pot, stir in 1/2 cup of frozen peas, and cook for another 5 minutes until the peas are heated through. Taste and adjust the seasoning with salt and pepper as needed. Serve the stew hot, garnished with fresh parsley if desired.

Ingredients List

  • For the Stew
    • 2 tablespoons olive oil (or vegetable oil)
    • 1 1/2 pounds bone-in, skinless chicken thighs
    • Salt and black pepper, to taste
    • 1 large yellow onion, diced
    • 2 cloves garlic, minced
    • 3 large carrots, peeled and sliced into 1/2-inch pieces
    • 3 medium potatoes (Yukon Gold or red potatoes)
    • 2 celery stalks, sliced
    • 1 parsnip, peeled and chopped (optional, for extra flavor)
    • 4 cups chicken broth (preferably low-sodium)
    • 1 cup water
    • 1/2 cup dry white wine (optional, for added depth)
    • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
    • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
    • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary (or fresh rosemary sprigs)
    • 1 bay leaf
    • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
    • 1 cup frozen peas or corn (optional)
    • Fresh parsley, chopped, for garnish
  • For Thickening (Optional)
    • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour or cornstarch
    • 1/4 cup water
