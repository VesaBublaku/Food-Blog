Berry Chia Overnight Oats

Servings: 1
Prep Time : 5 minutes
Cooling Time : 4-5 hours
Total Time : 4-5 hours

In a jar or container, combine the oats, chia seeds, milk, maple syrup, vanilla extract, and cinnamon (if using).

Stir well to ensure the chia seeds are evenly mixed, as they can clump together. Gently fold in the mixed berries, making sure they're distributed throughout the mixture.

Cover the container with a lid and refrigerate overnight, or for at least 4 hours, allowing the oats and chia seeds to absorb the liquid and soften.

In the morning, give the oats a quick stir, add a splash of milk if you'd like a looser consistency, and top with additional berries or a drizzle of honey if desired.

Enjoy straight from the jar for a convenient, nutritious breakfast!

Ingredients List

  • Base
    • 1/2 cup old-fashioned rolled oats
    • 1 tbsp chia seeds
    • 1/2 cup milk (any type: dairy, almond, oat, or soy)
  • Sweetener
    • 1-2 tsp maple syrup or honey (adjust to taste)
  • Flavorings
    • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
    • 1/4 tsp ground cinnamon (optional, for a warm flavor)
  • Berries
    • 1/4 cup fresh or frozen mixed berries
