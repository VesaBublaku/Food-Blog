Spicy Salmon Burgers

Servings: 2-3 servings
Prep Time : 25 minutes
Cook Time : 15 minutes
Total Time : 40 minutes

To make the Spicy Salmon Burgers, start by finely chopping the salmon or pulsing it in a food processor until it’s chunky but not a paste. In a large mixing bowl, combine the chopped salmon, panko breadcrumbs, red onion, Dijon mustard, mayonnaise, soy sauce, lemon juice, parsley, garlic powder, smoked paprika, cayenne pepper, salt, and black pepper, then add the egg and mix everything until well combined.

Form the mixture into 4 patties and refrigerate for at least 20 minutes to help them firm up. While the patties chill, mix the spicy aioli by combining mayonnaise, sriracha sauce, lemon juice, Dijon mustard, garlic powder, and a pinch of salt in a small bowl.

Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium-high heat and cook the patties for 4-5 minutes per side until golden brown and cooked through (internal temperature of 145°F/63°C).

While the patties cook, toast the burger buns until golden. To assemble, spread the spicy aioli on the bottom bun, then place the salmon patty on top, followed by lettuce, sliced tomato, and red onion, adding pickles if desired.

Serve immediately with your favorite side dish.

Ingredients List

  • For the Salmon Patties
    • 1 lb (450g) fresh or frozen wild-caught salmon
    • 1/4 cup panko breadcrumbs (or regular breadcrumbs)
    • 1/4 cup finely chopped red onion
    • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
    • 1 tablespoon mayonnaise
    • 1 tablespoon soy sauce (or tamari for gluten-free)
    • 1 tablespoon freshly squeezed lemon juice
    • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley (optional for freshness)
    • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
    • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
    • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper (adjust to desired spiciness)
    • Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste
    • 1 large egg
    • 1 tablespoon olive oil (for cooking)
  • For the Spicy Aioli
    • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
    • 1 tablespoon sriracha sauce
    • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
    • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
    • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
    • Salt, to taste
  • For Serving
    • 4 burger buns (preferably brioche or whole wheat)
    • Lettuce leaves
    • Sliced tomatoes
    • Sliced red onions
    • Pickles (optional)
