Bad Santa White Russian

Servings: 1
Prep Time : 5 minutes

To make a Bad Santa White Russian, fill an old-fashioned glass with ice, then pour in 2 oz of vodka, 1 oz of coffee liqueur, and 2 oz of eggnog (or cream if eggnog isnâ€™t available).

Stir everything together until well-mixed and chilled, then sprinkle a bit of ground cinnamon or nutmeg on top for a festive finish. For an extra touch, garnish with a cinnamon stick.

Enjoy immediately!

Ingredients List

  • For The Coctail
    • 2 oz (60 ml) vodka
    • 1 oz (30 ml) coffee liqueur
    • 1 oz (60 ml) eggnog
    • Ice (as needed for consistency)
  • Garnish
    • Ground cinnamon or nutmeg
    • Cinnamon stick
