Strawberry Shortcake Yogurt Bowls

Servings: 1-2
Prep Time : 10-15 minutes
Cook Time : 10 minutes
Total Time : 35-40 minutes

Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt. Add the cold, cubed butter and use a pastry cutter or your hands to work it into the flour until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs with pea-sized pieces of butter.

Pour in the milk and vanilla extract, and stir gently until just combined. Turn the dough out onto a floured surface, knead it gently 2-3 times, then pat it into a 1-inch thick rectangle and cut it into 6-8 squares or rounds.

Place the biscuits on the prepared baking sheet and bake for 12-15 minutes, or until golden brown. While the biscuits bake, toss the sliced strawberries with sugar and lemon juice, letting them sit for at least 10 minutes to release their juices.

For the whipped cream, beat the cold heavy cream in a chilled mixing bowl with an electric mixer until soft peaks form, then add powdered sugar and vanilla and beat until stiff peaks form. Once the biscuits have cooled slightly, cut them in half and place the bottom half in each bowl.

Top with macerated strawberries and their juices, add a dollop of whipped cream, and place the top half of the biscuit on top. Finish with more whipped cream and strawberries, and serve immediately with extra garnishes like fresh mint or a drizzle of honey if desired.

Ingredients List

  • For the Shortcake Biscuits
    • All-purpose flour – 1 1/2 cups
    • Baking powder – 2 teaspoons
    • Granulated sugar – 2 tablespoons
    • Salt – 1/4 teaspoon
    • Unsalted butter – 1/4 cup (cold and cubed)
    • Whole milk – 1/2 cup
    • Vanilla extract – 1 teaspoon
  • For the Strawberries
    • Fresh strawberries – 3 cups (hulled and sliced)
    • Granulated sugar – 2 tablespoons (for macerating the strawberries)
    • Lemon juice – 1 tablespoon (optional, for brightness)
  • For the Whipped Cream
    • Heavy cream – 1 cup (cold)
    • Powdered sugar – 2 tablespoons
    • Vanilla extract – 1 teaspoon
