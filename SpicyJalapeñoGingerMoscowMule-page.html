Spicy Jalapeño Ginger Moscow Mule

Servings: 1
Prep Time : 5 - 7 minutes

To make the Spicy Jalapeño Ginger Moscow Mule, muddle 1-2 slices of fresh jalapeño in a copper mug or glass

Add 2 oz vodka, 1/2 oz fresh lime juice, and ice. Top with 1 oz ginger beer and stir gently to combine.

Garnish with a lime wedge, a slice of jalapeño, and a sprig of fresh mint for a refreshing touch. Enjoy this fiery twist on the classic Moscow Mule!

Ingredients List

  • For The Drink
    • 2 oz Vodka
    • 1/2 oz Fresh lime juice
    • 1 oz Ginger beer
    • 1-2 slices Fresh jalapeño
    • Ice (for shaking)
  • Garnish
    • Lime wedge
    • Jalapeño slice
