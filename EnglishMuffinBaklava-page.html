English Muffin Baklava

Servings: 3-5
Prep Time : 10 minutes
Cook Time : 8-10 minutes
Total Time : 30-35 minutes

Start by preheating your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper. Split the English muffins in half and place them cut side up on the baking sheet, then brush each half with melted butter.

In a bowl, combine the finely chopped walnuts, pistachios, almonds, pecans (if using), sugar, cinnamon, ground cloves, and a pinch of salt, mixing well. Spoon about 1-2 tablespoons of the nut mixture onto each English muffin half, pressing gently so it adheres.

Bake in the preheated oven for 10-12 minutes until the muffins are golden and the nuts are lightly toasted. While the muffins bake, make the honey syrup by combining 1 cup honey, 1/2 cup water, 1/4 cup sugar, and a squeeze of lemon juice in a saucepan over medium heat.

Bring the mixture to a simmer, stirring until the sugar dissolves, and let it simmer for about 5 minutes until slightly thickened. Once the English muffins are out of the oven, immediately drizzle each one generously with the warm honey syrup, allowing it to soak in.

Let the English muffin baklava cool slightly, then serve warm or at room temperature, optionally garnished with extra chopped nuts or a sprinkle of cinnamon. Enjoy!

Ingredients List

  • For the Baklava Base
    • 6 English muffins (split in half, about 12 halves)
    • 1/2 cup unsalted butter (melted, for brushing)
  • For the Nut Filling
    • 1 cup walnuts (finely chopped)
    • 1 cup pistachios (finely chopped, shelled and unsalted)
    • 1/2 cup almonds (finely chopped)
    • 1/2 cup pecans (finely chopped, optional)
    • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
    • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
    • 1/4 tsp ground cloves (optional)
    • 1/4 tsp salt (optional, for balance)
  • For the Honey Syrup
    • 1 cup honey
    • 1/4 cup water
    • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
    • 1 tbsp lemon juice
    • 1 tsp vanilla extract (optional, for flavor)
    • 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon (optional, for extra warmth)
  • For Garnish
    • 1/4 cup chopped pistachios (for sprinkling)
    • 1/4 cup chopped walnuts (for sprinkling)
    • A sprinkle of cinnamon (optional)
