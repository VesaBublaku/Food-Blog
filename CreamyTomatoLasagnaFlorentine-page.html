Creamy Tomato Lasagna Florentine

Servings: 8 servings
Prep Time : 20 minutes
Cook Time : 1 hous
Total Time : 1 hour 20 minutes

Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). To make the sauce, heat 1 tbsp olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat, add the diced onion, and sauté for 5 minutes until softened.

Add 3 minced garlic cloves and cook for another minute. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, 1/2 cup heavy cream, and 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese, then season with 1 tsp dried basil, 1 tsp dried oregano, salt, and pepper.

Let the sauce simmer for 10-15 minutes to develop the flavors. Meanwhile, in a medium bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, egg, 1/2 cup grated Parmesan, chopped spinach, salt, and black pepper, mixing until smooth. To assemble, spread a thin layer of the tomato sauce on the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish, then add a layer of lasagna noodles.

Spread a layer of the ricotta mixture over the noodles, followed by a layer of sauce, then sprinkle with shredded mozzarella cheese. Repeat the layers (noodles, ricotta, sauce, mozzarella) until all ingredients are used, ending with a final layer of sauce and mozzarella on top.

Cover the dish with foil and bake for 30 minutes, then uncover and bake for an additional 15-20 minutes until bubbly and golden on top. Let the lasagna rest for 10 minutes before slicing, then garnish with fresh basil or parsley and serve.

Ingredients List

  • For the Sauce
    • 1 tbsp olive oil
    • 1 medium onion, diced
    • 3 garlic cloves, minced
    • 1 (28 oz) can crushed tomatoes
    • 1 (15 oz) can tomato sauce
    • 1/2 cup heavy cream
    • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
    • 1 tsp dried basil
    • 1 tsp dried oregano
    • Salt and pepper to taste
  • For the Ricotta Mixture
    • 1 (15 oz) container ricotta cheese
    • 1 egg
    • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
    • 2 cups fresh spinach, chopped
    • 1/2 tsp salt
    • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • For Assembly
    • 9-12 lasagna noodles, cooked according to package instructions
    • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
    • Fresh basil or parsley, chopped, for garnish
