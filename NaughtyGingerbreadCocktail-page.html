Naughty Gingerbread Cocktail

Servings: 1
Prep Time : 5 - 7 minutes

To make the Naughty Gingerbread Cocktail, begin by preparing the rim of your glass by rubbing the edge with a lemon wedge, then dipping it into a mixture of crushed graham crackers, cinnamon, and brown sugar

n a cocktail shaker, combine 2 oz spiced rum, 1 oz ginger liqueur, 1 oz heavy cream, 1/2 oz maple syrup, 1/2 oz fresh lemon juice, and 1/4 tsp ground cinnamon.

Add ice and shake vigorously for about 15-20 seconds. Fill your prepared glass with ice (optional), then strain the mixture into the glass. Garnish with a cinnamon stick and a sprinkle of grated nutmeg. Enjoy this cozy, spiced cocktail!

Ingredients List

  • For The Cocktail
    • 2 oz Spiced rum
    • 1 oz Ginger liqueur
    • 1 oz Heavy cream
    • 1/2 oz Maple syrup
    • 1/2 oz Fresh lemon juice
    • 1/4 tsp Ground cinnamon
    • Ice (for shaking)
  • For the Rim
    • 1/4 cup Crushed graham crackers
    • 1 tbsp Ground cinnamon
    • 1 tbsp Brown sugar
  • Garnish
    • Cinnamon stick
    • Grated nutmeg
