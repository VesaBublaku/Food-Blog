Mexican Casserole

Servings: 1-2
Prep Time : 10-15 minutes
Cook Time : 30-35 minutes
Total Time : 45-50 minutes

To make the Mexican casserole, start by heating olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat, then sauté the diced onion and minced garlic for 2-3 minutes until softened. Add the ground beef and cook until browned, breaking it apart as it cooks, then drain any excess fat.

Stir in the taco seasoning, salt, pepper, diced tomatoes, black beans, corn, and green chilies (if using), and simmer the mixture for 5-7 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together. While the beef mixture is simmering, lightly fry the flour tortillas in a separate skillet with olive oil for 1-2 minutes per side to soften them.

Once fried, set the tortillas aside to drain on paper towels. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C) and grease a 9x13-inch casserole dish. Start assembling the casserole by placing a layer of tortillas at the bottom of the dish, cutting them as needed to fit.

Top the tortillas with half of the beef mixture and then sprinkle with a generous amount of both cheddar and Monterey Jack cheeses. Repeat the layers with the remaining tortillas, beef mixture, and cheese.

Cover the casserole with aluminum foil and bake for 20 minutes, then remove the foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and golden brown. Let the casserole sit for 5 minutes before serving, and top with sour cream, chopped green onions, cilantro, and jalapeños for garnish.

Ingredients List

  • For the Casserole Base
    • Ground beef – 1 lb (or ground turkey for a leaner option)
    • Onion – 1 medium (diced)
    • Garlic – 2 cloves (minced)
    • Canned black beans – 1 can (15 oz), drained and rinsed
    • Canned corn kernels – 1 can (15 oz), drained
    • Canned diced tomatoes – 1 can (14.5 oz), drained
    • Green chilies – 1 can (4 oz), diced (optional for heat)
    • Taco seasoning – 2 tablespoons (or homemade seasoning)
    • Salt – 1/2 teaspoon
    • Black pepper – 1/4 teaspoon
  • For the Tortilla Layers
    • Flour tortillas – 6-8 large tortillas (or corn tortillas)
    • Olive oil – 1 tablespoon (for lightly frying the tortillas)
  • For the Cheese and Toppings
    • Shredded cheddar cheese – 1 1/2 cups
    • Shredded Monterey Jack cheese – 1 1/2 cups
    • Sour cream – 1/2 cup (optional, for serving)
    • Chopped green onions – 1/4 cup (for garnish)
    • Fresh cilantro – 1/4 cup (chopped, for garnish)
    • Sliced jalapeños – 2-3 (optional, for garnish)
