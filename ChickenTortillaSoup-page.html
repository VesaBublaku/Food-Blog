Chicken Tortilla Soup

Servings: 7 servings
Prep Time : 10-15 minutes
Cook Time : 15 minutes
Total Time : 45-55 minutes

Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and spread the tortilla strips on a baking sheet, drizzling with vegetable oil and tossing to coat them evenly, then bake for 10-12 minutes until golden and crispy, removing them from the oven and setting them aside.

In a large pot or Dutch oven, heat the olive oil over medium heat and sauté the chopped onion, carrot, and celery for 5-7 minutes until softened, then add the minced garlic and cook for an additional 1 minute.

Stir in the ground cumin, chili powder, smoked paprika (if using), and ground coriander (if using), cooking for 30 seconds to toast the spices. Pour in the chicken broth, followed by the diced tomatoes, green chilies (if using), and bay leaf, stirring everything together and bringing it to a simmer for 15 minutes.

Add the shredded cooked chicken, black beans (if using), and corn (if using), and simmer for another 5-10 minutes until heated through. Remove the bay leaf and stir in the lime juice, seasoning with salt and pepper to taste.

Ladle the soup into bowls and top with the crispy tortilla strips and your choice of optional toppings like sour cream, shredded cheese, avocado, cilantro, and lime wedges, then serve hot.

Ingredients List

  • For the Soup
    • 2 tablespoons olive oil
    • 1 medium yellow onion, chopped
    • 2 cloves garlic, minced
    • 1 medium carrot, peeled and diced
    • 1 small onion, finely chopped
    • 1 stalk celery, diced
    • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
    • 1 teaspoon chili powder
    • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika (optional, for extra depth of flavor)
    • 1/4 teaspoon ground coriander (optional)
    • 6 cups chicken broth (or vegetable broth for a vegetarian version)
    • 1 can (14.5 oz) diced tomatoes, undrained
    • 1 can (4 oz) green chilies (optional, for a slight heat)
    • 1 bay leaf
    • 3 cups shredded cooked chicken (rotisserie chicken works great)
    • 1 can (15 oz) black beans, drained and rinsed (optional)
    • 1 can (15 oz) corn kernels, drained (optional)
    • Salt and black pepper to taste
    • 1 tablespoon lime juice (freshly squeezed, for brightness)
  • For the Tortilla Strips
    • 4 corn tortillas, cut into thin strips
    • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
    • Salt, to taste
  • Optional Toppings
    • Sour cream or Greek yogurt
    • Shredded cheddar cheese or Monterey Jack cheese
    • Sliced avocado
    • Cilantro, chopped
    • Lime wedges
