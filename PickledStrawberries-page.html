Pickled Strawberries

Servings: 1 sandwich
Prep Time : 10 minutes
Total Time : 10 minutes

For a unique pickled strawberry sandwich, start by spreading a layer of cream cheese or goat cheese on slices of toasted bread, such as a hearty sourdough or whole grain

Layer the cream cheese with fresh arugula or spinach for a peppery bite, then add a generous amount of the pickled strawberries, ensuring theyâ€™re well-drained but still juicy. For extra texture and flavor, top the strawberries with a few slices of prosciutto or smoked turkey, and finish with a drizzle of honey or balsamic glaze for sweetness and tang.

Close the sandwich, press lightly, and enjoy the combination of savory, sweet, and tangy flavors.

Ingredients List

  • For the Sandwich
    • 2 slices of toasted bread
    • 2-3 tablespoons cream cheese or goat cheese
    • Fresh arugula or spinach
    • 1/4 to 1/2 cup pickled strawberries
    • Optional: prosciutto or smoked turkey
    • Honey or balsamic glaze for drizzling
