Sheet Pan Shrimp and Cauli Rice

Servings: 4-7 servings
Prep Time : 15 minutes
Cook Time : 15-20 minutes
Total Time : 30-35 minutes

reheat the oven to 400°F (200°C) and line a sheet pan with parchment paper or grease it lightly. In a bowl, toss the shrimp with olive oil, paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, cumin, cayenne pepper (if using), salt, and pepper until evenly coated, then set aside to marinate.

For the cauliflower rice, rice the cauliflower in a food processor or use pre-riced cauliflower. Heat 1 tablespoon olive oil in a skillet over medium heat, sauté the onion and garlic for 2-3 minutes until softened, then add the riced cauliflower, frozen peas (if using), salt, pepper, and thyme.

Cook for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the cauliflower is tender, then stir in lemon juice and set aside.

Spread the seasoned shrimp in a single layer on the sheet pan and bake for 8-10 minutes until pink, opaque, and cooked through.

Serve the shrimp over the cauliflower rice, drizzling with lemon juice and garnishing with chopped parsley.

Ingredients List

  • For the Shrimp
    • 1 lb large shrimp, peeled and deveined
    • 2 tablespoons olive oil
    • 1 teaspoon paprika
    • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
    • 1 teaspoon onion powder
    • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
    • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional, for heat)
    • Salt and black pepper, to taste
    • 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice (or more for serving)
    • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley (for garnish)
  • For the Cauliflower Rice
    • 1 medium head of cauliflower, riced
    • 1 tablespoon olive oil
    • 1 small onion, diced
    • 2 cloves garlic, minced
    • 1/2 cup frozen peas (optional)
    • Salt and pepper, to taste
    • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme (optional)
    • 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
    • 1 tablespoon fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)
