Christmas Pomegranate Punch

Servings: 4 - 6
Prep Time : 10 minutes

In a large pitcher or punch bowl, combine 1 1/2 cups pomegranate juice, 1 cup cranberry juice, 1 cup orange juice, and 1/2 cup vodka (if using)

Add 1/4 cup triple sec or orange liqueur for extra flavor, and sweeten with 1-2 tbsp of simple syrup, adjusting to taste. Just before serving, add 1 cup of sparkling water or club soda for fizz and stir gently

Pour over ice in individual glasses or keep ice in a separate bowl to avoid diluting the punch.

Garnish with pomegranate seeds, fresh cranberries, orange slices, and sprigs of rosemary. Enjoy this festive, vibrant Christmas punch!

Ingredients List

  • For The Punch
    • 1 1/2 cups Pomegranate juice
    • 1 cup Cranberry juice
    • 1 cup Orange juice
    • 1 cup Sparkling water or club soda
    • 1/2 cup Vodka
    • 1/4 cup Triple sec or orange liqueur
    • 1-2 tbsp Simple syrup
  • Garnish
    • Orange peel
    • Fresh pomegranate arils
    • Fresh cranberries
    • Sprigs of fresh rosemary
