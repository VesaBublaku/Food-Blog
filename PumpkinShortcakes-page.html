Pumpkin Shortcakes

Servings: 3-5
Prep Time : 20-25 minutes
Cook Time : 12-15 minutes
Total Time : 1 hour

Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, cinnamon, ginger (if using), and salt. Add the cold, cubed butter and use a pastry cutter or your hands to cut it into the dry ingredients until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.

In a separate bowl, whisk together the pumpkin puree, milk, egg, and vanilla extract, then add this mixture to the dry ingredients, stirring gently until just combined.

Turn the dough out onto a floured surface, gently pat it into a 1-inch thick rectangle, and cut out circles using a biscuit cutter. Place the shortcakes on the prepared baking sheet, brush with milk or an egg wash if desired, and bake for 12-15 minutes, until golden brown.

While the shortcakes bake, make the whipped cream by beating the heavy cream, powdered sugar, and vanilla until soft peaks form.

Once the shortcakes have cooled slightly, slice them in half, top with whipped cream, drizzle with caramel sauce, and sprinkle with cinnamon sugar if desired. Serve warm or at room temperature and enjoy!

Ingredients List

  • For the Shortcakes
    • 2 cups all-purpose flour, plus extra for dusting
    • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
    • 1 tablespoon baking powder
    • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
    • 1/4 teaspoon ground ginger (optional)
    • 1/4 teaspoon salt
    • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, cold and cubed
    • 3/4 cup canned pumpkin puree (not pumpkin pie filling)
    • 1/4 cup whole milk
    • 1 large egg
    • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • For the Whipped Cream
    • 1 cup heavy whipping cream
    • 1 tablespoon powdered sugar
    • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Optional Garnishes
    • Caramel sauce (store-bought or homemade)
    • Cinnamon sugar (for sprinkling)
