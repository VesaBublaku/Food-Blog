Cauliflower Black Bean Tostadas

Servings: 4 servings
Prep Time : 10 minutes
Cook Time : 35 minutes
Total Time : 45 minutes

To make cauliflower black bean tostadas, start by preheating your oven to 400°F (200°C).

On a baking sheet, toss 1 medium cauliflower (cut into small florets) with 2 tablespoons olive oil, 1 teaspoon cumin, 1 teaspoon paprika, 1/2 teaspoon chili powder, salt, and pepper.

Roast the cauliflower for 20-25 minutes, flipping halfway through, until tender and slightly caramelized. While the cauliflower roasts, heat 1 tablespoon olive oil in a pan over medium heat and sauté 1 small onion (chopped) for 4-5 minutes until soft.

Add 2 cloves of minced garlic and cook for 1 more minute. Stir in 1 can of drained and rinsed black beans, 1/2 teaspoon cumin, 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika, and a pinch of salt. Heat the beans through, then set aside.

For the tostadas, brush 6 corn tortillas lightly with olive oil and bake them on a separate baking sheet in the oven for 8-10 minutes, flipping once, until crispy and golden.

To assemble, spread a layer of the black bean mixture on each tostada, top with the roasted cauliflower, and garnish with chopped cilantro, a squeeze of lime, and optional toppings like avocado slices, crumbled queso fresco, or salsa. Serve immediately.

Ingredients List

  • For the Tostadas
    • 1 medium cauliflower, cut into florets
    • 2 tablespoons olive oil
    • 1 teaspoon cumin
    • 1 teaspoon paprika
    • Salt and pepper to taste
    • 1 small onion, chopped
    • 2 cloves garlic, minced
    • 1 can black beans
    • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
    • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
    • 6 corn tortillas
    • Optional toppings: cilantro, lime wedges, avocado, queso fresco
