Instant Pot Mac and Cheese

Servings: 6 - 8
Prep Time : 5 minutes
Cook Time : 10 - 15 minutes
Total Time : 15 -20 minutes

For a creamy Instant Pot mac and cheese, start by adding 1 pound of elbow macaroni, 4 cups of water, 1 teaspoon of salt, and 1 tablespoon of butter to the Instant Pot.

Close the lid, set the valve to sealing, and cook on high pressure for 4 minutes. Once the cooking time is up, perform a quick release to release the pressure. Open the lid, and stir in 2 cups of shredded sharp cheddar cheese, 1/2 cup of grated Parmesan cheese, 1/2 cup of milk (whole milk or heavy cream works best for extra creaminess), and 1 tablespoon of Dijon mustard (optional for extra flavor).

Stir until the cheese melts completely and the sauce is smooth. Adjust seasoning with additional salt and pepper to taste. For an extra creamy texture, you can also add 1/4 cup of cream cheese. Serve immediately for a rich and comforting dish.

Ingredients List

  • For the Pasta
    • 1 pound elbow macaroni
    • 4 cups water
    • 1 teaspoon salt
    • 1 tablespoon butter
    • 2 cups shredded sharp cheddar cheese
    • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
    • 1/2 cup milk
    • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
    • Salt and pepper to taste
    • Optional: 1/4 cup cream cheese
