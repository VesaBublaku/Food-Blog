Roasted Mushroom Sandwich

Servings: 2 sandwiches
Prep Time : 15 minutes
Cook Time : 25 - 30 minutes
Total Time : 40 - 45 minutes

To elevate the roasted mushroom sandwich further, try adding some caramelized onions and creamy avocado.

Start by thinly slicing a large onion, then cook it in a skillet with a bit of olive oil over medium heat, stirring occasionally until golden and caramelized (about 20-25 minutes). For extra richness, add a splash of balsamic vinegar during the last few minutes of cooking.

Meanwhile, mash half of a ripe avocado with a pinch of salt and a squeeze of lemon juice. When assembling, spread the mashed avocado on one side of the toasted bread and the aioli or mayonnaise on the other.

Layer on the roasted mushrooms, caramelized onions, a few slices of tomato if desired, and some peppery arugula or spinach. For an extra twist, you could add a sprinkle of truffle salt, fresh basil, or a handful of microgreens.

Close the sandwich, press it slightly, and cut it in half to serve warm. This makes a perfect savory meal with complex flavors and textures.

Ingredients List

  • For the Sandwich
    • 1 pound mixed mushrooms
    • 2 tablespoons olive oil
    • 2 garlic cloves, minced
    • 1 teaspoon fresh thyme leaves
    • Salt and black pepper, to taste
    • 1 large onion, thinly sliced
    • 1/2 ripe avocado
    • Squeeze of lemon juice
    • 4 slices of sourdough or ciabatta bread, toasted
    • 1/2 cup fresh arugula or spinach
