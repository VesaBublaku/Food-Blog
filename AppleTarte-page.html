Apple Tarte

Servings: 6-8
Prep Time : 30 minutes
Cook Time : 45-50 minutes
Total Time : 1 hour 20 minutes

To make this apple tart, start with the crust. In a medium bowl, combine the flour, sugar, and salt.

Add the chilled, cubed butter and cut it into the flour mixture using a pastry cutter or your fingers until it resembles coarse crumbs. Slowly add ice water, 1 tablespoon at a time, stirring gently until the dough just begins to come together. Avoid overworking the dough, as this keeps the crust tender

Once it forms a cohesive dough, shape it into a disk, wrap it in plastic wrap, and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.

While the dough chills, prepare the apple filling. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). In a small bowl, mix together the granulated sugar, brown sugar, cinnamon, and nutmeg (if using) and set it aside. Place the apple slices in a large bowl, pour the melted butter over them, and toss gently. Sprinkle the sugar mixture over the apples, and toss them again to coat evenly.

Once the dough has chilled, roll it out on a lightly floured surface to about ¼ inch thick. Carefully transfer it to a 9-inch tart pan with a removable bottom, pressing the dough gently into the edges. Trim any excess dough from the edges for a clean finish

Arrange the apple slices in an overlapping circular pattern, starting from the outer edge of the tart and working inward to the center, creating a spiral design. Sprinkle any leftover sugar mixture over the apples.

Place the tart on a baking sheet and bake it in the preheated oven for 45-50 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the apples are tender and caramelized. Once the tart is done, remove it from the oven and let it cool slightly for 10-15 minutes.

For the finishing glaze, heat the apricot jam and water together in a small saucepan over low heat, stirring until smooth. Alternatively, you can microwave the mixture for 10-15 seconds to melt the jam. Using a pastry brush, gently apply the glaze over the apples to give them a beautiful shine and extra sweetness.

Finally, slice the tart and serve it warm or at room temperature. A scoop of vanilla ice cream or a dollop of whipped cream on the side makes a perfect addition to this elegant dessert. Enjoy!

Ingredients List

  • Tart Crust
    • 1 ¼ cups all-purpose flour
    • 1 tablespoon granulated sugar
    • ½ teaspoon salt
    • ½ cup unsalted butter, chilled and cubed
    • 2-4 tablespoons ice water
  • Apple Filling
    • 5 medium apples
    • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
    • ⅓ cup granulated sugar
    • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
    • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamons
    • ¼ teaspoon ground nutmeg (optional)
  • Glaze
    • ¼ cup apricot jam or apple jelly
    • 1 tablespoon water
