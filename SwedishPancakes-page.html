Swedish Pancakes

Servings: 4 (makes about 8 pancakes)
Prep Time : 10 minutes
Cook Time : 20 minutes
Total Time : 30 minutes

For classic Swedish pancakes, whisk together 1 cup of all-purpose flour, 1 tablespoon of sugar, 1/2 teaspoon of salt, and 2 large eggs in a mixing bowl.

Gradually add 1 1/4 cups of milk, whisking continuously to create a smooth batter with a thin consistency. For extra richness, add 1 tablespoon of melted butter to the batter and stir.

Preheat a nonstick skillet or a crepe pan over medium heat, and add a little butter or oil to coat. Pour in about 1/4 cup of batter, tilting the pan to spread it into a thin layer. Cook for 1-2 minutes until the edges start to lift, then flip and cook for another 1-2 minutes on the other side until golden.

Repeat with the remaining batter, stacking the pancakes to keep them warm. Serve with traditional toppings like lingonberry jam, a sprinkle of powdered sugar, or a dollop of whipped cream, and enjoy this light, tender treat!

Ingredients List

  • For the Batter
    • 1 cup all-purpose flour
    • 1 tablespoon sugar
    • 1/2 teaspoon salt
    • 2 large eggs
    • 1 1/4 cups milk
    • 1 tablespoon melted butter
