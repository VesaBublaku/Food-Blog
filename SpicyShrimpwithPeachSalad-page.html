Spicy Shrimp with Peach Salad

Servings: 4
Prep Time : 10 minutes
Cook Time : 10 minutes
Total Time : 20 minutes

Season 1 lb of peeled and deveined shrimp with 1 teaspoon paprika, 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper, 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder, salt, and pepper.

Heat 1 tablespoon olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat and cook the shrimp for 2-3 minutes per side until theyâ€™re pink and cooked through. While the shrimp cooks, prepare the salad by slicing 2 ripe peaches, then tossing them in a bowl with 4 cups mixed greens, 1/4 cup thinly sliced red onion, 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese, and 2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro.

Drizzle the salad with a dressing made from 1 tablespoon olive oil, 1 tablespoon lime juice, 1 teaspoon honey, salt, and pepper to taste, then toss gently to combine.

Serve the spicy shrimp on top of the peach salad and garnish with additional cilantro if desired.

Ingredients List

  • For the Spicy Shrimp
    • 1 lb peeled and deveined shrimp
    • 1 teaspoon paprika
    • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
    • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
    • Salt and pepper, to taste
    • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • For the Peach Salad
    • 2 ripe peaches, sliced
    • 4 cups mixed greens
    • 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced
    • 1/4 cup feta cheese, crumbled
    • 2 tablespoons fresh cilantro, chopped
  • For the Dressing
    • 1 tablespoon olive oil
    • 1 tablespoon lime juice
    • 1 teaspoon honey
    • Salt and pepper,to taste
