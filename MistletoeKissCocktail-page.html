Mistletoe Kiss Cocktail

Servings: 1
Prep Time : 5 - 7 minutes

To make the Mistletoe Kiss Cocktail, combine 1 1/2 oz vodka, 1 oz cranberry juice, 1/2 oz Chambord, 1/2 oz fresh lime juice, and 1/4 oz simple syrup in a cocktail shaker

Add ice and shake vigorously for about 15-20 seconds. Strain the mixture into a chilled cocktail glass.

Garnish with fresh cranberries, a lime wedge, and a sprig of rosemary for a festive touch. Sip and enjoy this sweet and tart holiday cocktail!

Ingredients List

  • For The Cocktail
    • 1 1/2 oz Vodka
    • 1 oz Cranberry juice
    • 1/2 oz Chambord (raspberry liqueur)
    • 1/2 oz Fresh lime juice
    • 1/4 oz Simple syrup
    • Ice (for shaking)
  • For the Garnish
    • Lime wedges
    • Sprig of rosemary
    • Fresh cranberries
