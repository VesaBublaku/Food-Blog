Danish Pastry

Servings: 4-5
Prep Time : 10 minutes
Cook Time : 15-20 minutes
Total Time : 3-4 hours

To make Danish pastry, first prepare the dough by mixing flour, sugar, salt, and yeast in a large bowl, then add softened butter, lukewarm milk, and an egg, stirring until combined; knead the dough on a lightly floured surface for about 5 minutes until smooth, then shape it into a ball, place it in a bowl, cover, and let it rise in a warm spot for about 1 hour or until doubled in size.

While the dough rises, prepare the butter block by placing the cold butter between two sheets of parchment paper, then pounding and rolling it into a square about 6 inches (15 cm) wide; refrigerate it until needed.

After the dough has risen, roll it out on a floured surface into a 12-inch (30 cm) square, place the butter block in the center, and fold the dough’s edges over the butter to fully encase it, pinching the seams to seal. Roll the dough into a long rectangle, then fold it into thirds, like a letter, wrap it in plastic wrap, and chill in the fridge for 30 minutes; repeat this process of rolling, folding, and chilling two more times to create layers./p>

After the final fold and chilling, roll the dough out to about 1/4-inch (0.6 cm) thickness and cut into squares. Place a small amount of your chosen filling (such as fruit jam, cream cheese mixture, or almond paste) in the center of each square, then fold two opposite corners toward the center to make a traditional Danish shape, pressing gently to seal.

Transfer pastries to a baking sheet lined with parchment paper, cover lightly, and let them rise for 30-45 minutes. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C) and brush the pastries with an egg wash made from one beaten egg mixed with a little milk, then bake for 15-20 minutes. Enjoy!

Ingredients List

  • Dough Ingredients
    • 500 grams all-purpose flour
    • 60 grams granulated sugar
    • 10 grams (about 1 1/2 teaspoons) salt
    • 10 grams instant yeast (or active dry yeast)
    • 50 grams unsalted butter, softened
    • 200 ml whole milk, lukewarm
    • 1 large egg, room temperature
  • Filling Options
    • 150 grams (e.g., raspberry, apricot, or strawberry) fruit jam
    • 100 grams cream cheese
    • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
    • 30 grams sugar
    • 100 grams almond paste or marzipan
  • Egg Wash
    • 1 egg, beaten
    • 1 tablespoon milk
  • Optional Toppings
    • Powdered Sugar for dusting
    • Icing
    • Slivered Almonds for a crunchy topping
