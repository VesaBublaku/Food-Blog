Japchae

Servings: 4 sandwiches
Prep Time : 15 minutes
Cook Time : 15 - 20 minutes
Total Time : 30 - 35 minutes

To make Japchae, begin by boiling 200g of sweet potato noodles according to the package instructions until soft, then drain, rinse under cold water, and cut the noodles into shorter lengths. Toss them with 1 tbsp of sesame oil to prevent sticking and set aside.

In a small bowl, mix 4 tbsp soy sauce, 1 1/2 tbsp sugar, and 1 tbsp sesame oil to make the sauce.

If using beef, stir-fry 100g of thinly sliced beef in a bit of oil over medium heat until browned, seasoning with salt and pepper, then remove and set aside. In the same pan, add 1/2 thinly sliced onion, 1 julienned carrot, 1 red bell pepper, and 4-5 sliced shiitake mushrooms, stir-frying for 2-3 minutes until the vegetables are softened but still crisp.

Add 2 green onions and cook for another minute. In a large bowl, combine the cooked noodles, beef (if using), stir-fried vegetables, and 2 cups of blanched spinach, then pour the sauce over the mixture.

Toss everything gently until well combined, and adjust the seasoning with salt and pepper as needed. Finally, sprinkle sesame seeds over the dish and serve warm or at room temperature.

Ingredients List

  • For the Noodles and Sauce
    • 8 oz sweet potato glass noodles
    • 3 tbsp soy sauce
    • 1 tbsp sesame oil
    • 1 tbsp sugar
    • 1/2 tbsp mirin
    • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • For the Stir-Fry
    • 100g beef
    • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
    • 1/2 lb beef
    • 1 carrot, julienned
    • 1/2 onion
    • 1 bell pepper
    • 1 cup spinach leaves
    • 4-5 shiitake mushtooms
    • 2 green onions
