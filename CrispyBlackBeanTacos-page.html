Crispy Black Bean Tacos

Servings: 4(2-3 tacos per person)
Prep Time : 10 minutes
Cook Time : 15 - 20 minutes
Total Time : 25 - 30 minutes

To make Crispy Black Bean Tacos, start by heating a tablespoon of oil in a skillet over medium heat and add a can of drained black beans, along with 1 teaspoon of cumin, 1/2 teaspoon of chili powder, and a pinch of salt.

Cook for 3-5 minutes, mashing some of the beans with a fork to create a chunky texture. Warm small corn tortillas briefly, then place a spoonful of the black bean mixture and a sprinkle of shredded cheese on one half of each tortilla.

Fold the tortillas over to create half-moon shapes, pressing gently. In a separate large skillet, heat a thin layer of oil over medium-high heat, then cook the tacos for 2-3 minutes on each side until theyâ€™re golden and crispy. Drain on a paper towel, and serve warm with toppings like shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, avocado, salsa, or sour cream for a deliciously crunchy meal.

Ingredients List

  • For the Filling
    • 1 tbsp olive oil
    • 1 can (15 oz) black beans, drained and rinsed
    • 1 tsp ground cumin
    • 1 tsp chili powder
    • 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
    • 8-10 small corn tortillas
    • Salt and pepper, to taste
    • 1 cup shredded cheese
  • Toppings
    • Shredded lettuce
    • Diced tomatoes
    • Avocado slices
    • Salsa
    • Sour cream or Greek yogurt
