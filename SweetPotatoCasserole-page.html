Sweet Potato Casserole

Servings: 4-9
Prep Time : 15-20 minutes
Cook Time : 25-30 minutes
Total Time : 1-1 hour 25 minutes

Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a 9x13-inch baking dish. Peel and cube the sweet potatoes, then place them in a large pot and cover with water. Bring to a boil and cook for 10-15 minutes, or until the sweet potatoes are tender.

Drain and mash the sweet potatoes in the pot. Stir in the melted butter, brown sugar, milk (or cream), beaten eggs, vanilla extract, cinnamon, nutmeg, salt, and maple syrup (if using) until smooth and well-combined.

In a separate bowl, make the streusel topping by combining flour, brown sugar, cinnamon, and salt. Cut in the cold butter using a pastry cutter, fork, or your hands until the mixture forms coarse crumbs.

Stir in the chopped pecans or walnuts, if using. Spread the sweet potato mixture into the prepared baking dish and sprinkle the streusel topping evenly over the top.

Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the topping is golden brown and the casserole is heated through. Let it cool slightly before serving.

Ingredients List

  • For the Sweet Potato Base
    • 4 large sweet potatoes, peeled and cubed (about 4-5 cups)
    • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
    • 1/2 cup brown sugar, packed
    • 1/2 cup whole milk or heavy cream (for a richer texture)
    • 2 large eggs, beaten
    • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
    • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
    • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
    • 1/4 teaspoon salt
    • 1 tablespoon maple syrup (optional, for extra flavor)
  • For the Caramel Filling
    • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
    • 1 cup packed light brown sugar
    • 1/2 cup heavy cream
    • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
    • 1/4 teaspoon salt
    • 1 1/2 cups chopped pecans, toasted
  • For the Streusel Topping
    • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
    • 1/2 cup brown sugar, packed
    • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
    • 1/4 teaspoon salt
    • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, cold and cut into small pieces
    • 1/2 cup chopped pecans or walnuts (optional)
