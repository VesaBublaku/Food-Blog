Banana Nutella Crepe

Servings: 3-5
Prep Time : 15-30 minutes
Cook Time : 2-3 minutes
Total Time : 20-25 minutes

In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, and salt. In a separate bowl, combine the milk, eggs, melted butter, and vanilla extract, then pour this into the dry ingredients, whisking until smooth.

Let the batter rest for 15-30 minutes. Heat a non-stick skillet or crepe pan over medium heat and lightly grease with butter or oil. Pour about 1/4 cup of batter into the pan, swirling to form a thin layer, and cook for 1-2 minutes until the edges lift and turn golden.

Flip the crepe and cook for another minute, then transfer to a plate. Repeat with the remaining batter.

Spread a thin layer of Nutella over half of each crepe and add a few banana slices. Fold the crepe in half, then into quarters.

Dust with powdered sugar, add whipped cream if desired, sprinkle with chopped hazelnuts or almonds, and drizzle with chocolate or caramel sauce. Serve warm, garnished with fresh mint leaves if desired.

Ingredients List

  • Crepe Batter
    • All-purpose flour – 1 cup
    • Milk – 1 cup (preferably whole milk for a rich texture)
    • Eggs – 2 large
    • Melted butter – 2 tablespoons
    • Granulated sugar – 1 tablespoon
    • Salt – a pinch
    • Vanilla extract – 1/2 teaspoon
  • Filling
    • Nutella – 1/4 cup (or more to taste)
    • Bananas – 2 medium (sliced thinly)
  • Optional Toppings
    • Whipped cream – for serving
    • Powdered sugar – for dusting
    • Chopped hazelnuts or sliced almonds – 2 tablespoons (for crunch)
    • Fresh mint leaves – for garnish
    • Chocolate sauce or caramel sauce – for drizzling
