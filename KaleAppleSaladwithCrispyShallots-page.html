Kale Apple Salad with Crispy Shallots

Servings: 4
Prep Time : 10 minutes
Cook Time : 10 minutes
Total Time : 20 minutes

For a flavorful kale apple salad with crispy shallots, start by preparing the shallots.

Thinly slice 2 shallots and fry them in a small pan with about 1/4 cup of vegetable oil over medium heat until golden and crispy, about 5-7 minutes. Remove the shallots from the oil and drain on a paper towel, seasoning them with a pinch of salt.

Next, for the salad, wash and dry 4 cups of kale, removing the stems and tearing the leaves into bite-sized pieces. In a large bowl, massage the kale with 1 tablespoon of olive oil and a pinch of salt to soften it. Slice 2 apples (such as Honeycrisp or Granny Smith) into thin wedges and add them to the kale.

For the dressing, whisk together 2 tablespoons of olive oil, 1 tablespoon of apple cider vinegar, 1 teaspoon of Dijon mustard, 1 teaspoon of honey, salt, and pepper to taste. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss to coat evenly.

Top the salad with the crispy shallots, a sprinkle of toasted pumpkin seeds or walnuts, and optional crumbled goat cheese for added richness. Serve immediately for a refreshing, crunchy, and slightly sweet salad.

Ingredients List

  • For the Salad
    • 4 cups kale
    • 2 apples
    • Optional: toasted pumpkin seeds, walnuts
  • For the Crispy Shallots
    • 2 shallots, thinly sliced
    • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
    • Pinch of salt
  • For the Dressing
    • 2 tablespoons olive oil
    • 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
    • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
    • 1 teaspoon honey
    • Salt and pepper, to taste
