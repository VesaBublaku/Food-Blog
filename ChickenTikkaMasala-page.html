Chicken Tikka Masala

Servings: 4
Prep Time : 30 minutes
Cook Time : 30 - 40 minutes
Total Time : 1 hour

o begin, prepare the chicken by combining yogurt, lemon juice, minced garlic, grated ginger, ground cumin, coriander, turmeric, garam masala, chili powder, and salt in a large bowl.

Add the chicken pieces, mixing until well coated, then cover and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes (or up to 2 hours) to allow the flavors to meld. Once marinated, heat your grill or grill pan to medium-high heat. Thread the marinated chicken onto skewers (if using a grill) or place directly onto the grill pan

Grill the chicken for 5-7 minutes on each side, or until fully cooked and slightly charred, ensuring it’s tender and juicy. If using a broiler, place the chicken on a baking sheet and broil for 10-12 minutes, flipping halfway through for even cooking. While the chicken cooks, prepare the sauce by heating olive oil or ghee in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and cook for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally until the onion softens and turns golden brown. Then, add the garlic and ginger and sauté for another minute until fragrant. Stir in the ground spices—cumin, coriander, turmeric, garam masala, and chili powder—and cook for 1-2 minutes, allowing the spices to toast and become aromatic.

Next, pour in the crushed tomatoes and bring the mixture to a simmer. Let the sauce cook for 10-15 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld and the sauce to thicken slightly. Add the heavy cream and water (or chicken broth), stirring to incorporate, and simmer for a few more minutes. Once the chicken is cooked, add it to the sauce and stir gently to coat the chicken in the creamy, spiced tomato sauce.

Let everything simmer for an additional 5-10 minutes, allowing the chicken to absorb the rich flavors of the sauce. Finally, garnish with fresh cilantro before serving. This flavorful Chicken Tikka Masala pairs perfectly with steamed rice or naan bread, making for a complete, satisfying meal.

Ingredients List

  • For the Chicken Marinad
    • 1 lb chicken breast or thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces
    • 1 cup plain yogurt
    • 2 tbsp lemon juice
    • 2 cloves garlic, minced
    • 1 tbsp grated ginger
    • 1 tsp ground cumin
    • 1 tsp ground coriander
    • 1 tsp ground turmeric
    • 1 tsp garam masala
    • ½ tsp chili powder
    • Salt and pepper
  • For the Tikka Masala Sauce
    • 2 tbsp olive oil or ghee
    • 1 large onion
    • 2 cloves garlic, minced
    • 1 tbsp grated ginger
    • 1 can (14 oz) crushed tomatoes
    • 1 tsp ground cumin
    • Lemon wedges, for serving
    • 1 tsp ground coriander
    • 1 tsp ground turmeric
    • 1 cup heavy cream
    • ½ cup water or chicken broth
    • Fresh cilantro
