Honey Chipotle Chicken Skewers

Servings: 4
Prep Time : 40 minutes
Cook Time : 15 minutes
Total Time : 1 hour 15 minutes

In a large bowl, whisk together 2 tablespoons olive oil, 1 tablespoon honey, 2 tablespoons minced chipotle peppers in adobo sauce, 1 teaspoon garlic powder, 1 teaspoon smoked paprika, and 1 tablespoon lime juice, then season with salt and pepper to taste.

Add 1 1/2 lbs cubed boneless, skinless chicken breasts to the marinade and toss to coat. Cover and refrigerate the chicken for at least 30 minutes, or up to 2 hours to allow the flavors to meld. While the chicken marinates, prepare your skewers by threading the marinated chicken, red and yellow bell pepper chunks, and red onion chunks onto skewers.

Preheat the grill or grill pan to medium-high heat. Grill the skewers for about 4-5 minutes per side, or until the chicken is cooked through and has a nice char, ensuring the internal temperature of the chicken reaches 165Â°F.

Once done, remove the skewers from the grill and serve immediately with extra lime wedges on the side.

Ingredients List

  • For the Chicken
    • 1 1/2 lbs boneless, skinless chicken breasts
    • 2 tablespoons olive oil
    • 1 tablespoon honey
    • 2 tablespoons chipotle peppers in adobo sauce
    • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
    • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
    • 1 tablespoon lime juice
    • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • For the Skewers
    • 1 red bell pepper, cut into chunks
    • 1 yellow bell pepper, cut into chunks
    • 1 red onion, cut into chunks
