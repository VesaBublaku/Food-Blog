Pomegranate Braised Short Ribs

Servings: 4-6
Prep Time : 30 minutes
Cook Time : 2.5- 3 hours
Total Time : 3 - 3.5 hours

To make Pomegranate Braised Short Ribs, start by preheating your oven to 350°F (175°C). Season the short ribs generously with salt and pepper

In a large, heavy-bottomed ovenproof pot, heat olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the short ribs in batches and sear them on all sides until deeply browned, about 4-5 minutes per side. Remove the ribs and set them aside. In the same pot, lower the heat to medium and add the chopped onion

Cook for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally, until softened and translucent. Add the minced garlic and cook for another minute until fragrant, then stir in the tomato paste and cook for 2-3 minutes to deepen its flavor.

Next, pour in the red wine, scraping the browned bits from the bottom of the pot, and let it reduce for 3-4 minutes. Add the pomegranate juice, pomegranate molasses, and beef broth, along with brown sugar if you prefer a sweeter sauce. Return the short ribs to the pot, making sure they’re submerged in the liquid

Add the cinnamon stick, rosemary, and thyme, and bring the mixture to a simmer. Cover the pot with a tight-fitting lid and transfer it to the oven. Braise the ribs for 2.5 to 3 hours, or until the meat is tender and falls off the bone. Once the ribs are done, remove them from the pot and discard the herb sprigs and cinnamon stick.

If the sauce needs thickening, simmer it on the stovetop for 5-10 minutes until it reaches your desired consistency. Stir in fresh lemon juice and adjust seasoning with salt and pepper. To serve, arrange the short ribs on a platter and spoon the pomegranate sauce over the top

Garnish with fresh pomegranate seeds and chopped parsley. Serve with mashed potatoes, rice, or roasted vegetables to complete the dish.

Ingredients List

  • For the Short Ribs
    • 4-5 lbs beef short ribs (bone-in)
    • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
    • 2 tablespoons olive oil
    • 1 medium onion, chopped
    • 4 cloves garlic, minced
    • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
    • 1 cup pomegranate juice
    • 1/4 cup pomegranate molasses
    • 1 cup beef broth (or stock)
    • 1 cup dry red wine
    • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
    • 2 sprigs fresh rosemary
    • 2-3 sprigs fresh thyme
    • 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
    • Pomegranate seeds
    • Pomegranate seeds
