Frozen Blueberry Paloma

Servings: 1
Prep Time : 5 minutes

In a blender, combine the tequila, frozen blueberries, grapefruit juice, lime juice, and simple syrup.

Blend until smooth and slushy. Pour into a tall glass, leaving room to top with a splash of club soda for a bit of fizz. Garnish with a lime wheel and a few extra blueberries for presentation.

This cocktail is quick to prepare and only takes about 5 minutes, making it easy to whip up a batch for guests or a refreshing treat for yourself.

Ingredients List

  • Base Spirits
    • 2 oz tequila
  • Fruit and Juice
    • 1 cup frozen blueberries
    • 2 oz fresh grapefruit juice
    • 1 oz fresh lime juice
  • Sweetness & Fruit
    • 1 oz simple syrup
  • Sparkling
    • Club soda
  • Garnish
    • Lime wheel and blueberries
