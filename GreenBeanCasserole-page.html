Green Bean Casserole

Servings: 4-6 servings
Prep Time : 15-20 minutes
Cook Time : 20-25 minutes
Total Time : 50-60 minutes

Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a 9x13-inch casserole dish. Start by preparing the green beans; if using fresh beans, trim the ends and cut them into bite-sized pieces, then blanch them by boiling in salted water for 3-4 minutes until they are tender yet still crisp. Drain and set aside. If using canned green beans, simply drain them well.

In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium heat, then add the chopped onion and cook for 3-4 minutes until it becomes soft and translucent. Add the minced garlic and cook for another 30 seconds, ensuring it doesn’t burn. Stir in the mushroom soup and milk, and let the mixture come to a gentle simmer, mixing well to combine.

Season with salt, pepper, and thyme (if using), and stir in sour cream if you'd like a richer, creamier sauce. Allow the sauce to cook for another 2-3 minutes to thicken slightly. Next, add the cooked green beans to the skillet, stirring to coat them evenly with the creamy mushroom sauce. Once the beans are well mixed with the sauce, transfer everything into the prepared casserole dish, spreading it out evenly. For the topping, sprinkle the crispy fried onions over the top of the casserole, making sure they cover the entire surface.

If you’re using shredded cheddar cheese, add it on top of the onions for an extra layer of flavor. Place the casserole in the preheated oven and bake for 20-25 minutes until the topping is golden brown and crispy and the casserole is heated through. If you prefer an even crispier topping, you can turn on the broiler for the last 2-3 minutes of baking, but be careful to watch closely to prevent burning.

Once done, remove the casserole from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving. This delicious green bean casserole is a perfect side dish that’s rich in flavor with a wonderful creamy texture and a crispy topping.

Ingredients List

  • For the Casserole
    • 4 cups fresh green beans (or 2 cans of green beans)
    • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter
    • 1 small yellow onion, finely chopped
    • 2 cloves garlic, minced
    • 2 cups mushroom soup (use homemade or canned)
    • 1/2 cup milk
    • 1/2 cup sour cream (optional, for added richness)
    • 1/2 teaspoon salt
    • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
    • 1/4 teaspoon ground thyme (optional, for extra flavor)
  • For the Topping
    • 1 1/2 cups crispy fried onions (store-bought or homemade)
    • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese (optional)
