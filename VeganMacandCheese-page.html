Vegan Mac and Cheese

Servings: 7 servings
Prep Time : 10- minutes
Cook Time : 5-10
Total Time : 30-35 minutes

Begin by placing 1 cup of raw cashews in a bowl, covering them with hot water, and soaking for at least 15 minutes to soften (or soak in cold water for 2-4 hours if time permits); after soaking, drain and rinse the cashews. In a large pot, bring salted water to a boil and cook 12 ounces of elbow macaroni according to package instructions until al dente, then drain and return the pasta to the pot.

To make the vegan cheese sauce, combine the soaked cashews, 1 1/2 cups unsweetened almond milk, 1/2 cup nutritional yeast, 2 tablespoons tahini (optional), 1 tablespoon lemon juice, 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar, 2 tablespoons olive oil, 1 teaspoon garlic powder, 1 teaspoon onion powder, 1 teaspoon mustard, 1/2 teaspoon turmeric (optional), 1 teaspoon salt, 1/2 teaspoon black pepper, and 1/4 teaspoon smoked paprika (optional) in a high-speed blender; blend until smooth and creamy, scraping down the sides as needed, and adjust seasoning to taste.

Pour the cheese sauce over the cooked pasta in the pot, place the pot over low heat, and stir continuously for 3-5 minutes until the pasta is evenly coated and the sauce is heated through, adding a splash of almond milk if needed to reach your desired consistency.

Serve the vegan mac and cheese hot, garnishing with freshly chopped parsley or chives, a sprinkle of paprika or smoked paprika, and toasted breadcrumbs for added texture, if desired.

This Vegan Mac and Cheese is creamy, cheesy, and totally satisfying—perfect for a comforting meal that’s full of flavor without any dairy!

Ingredients List

  • For the Pasta
    • 12 ounces elbow macaroni (or other pasta of choice)
    • Salt, for pasta water
  • For the Sauce
    • 1 cup raw cashews (soaked in hot water for at least 15 minutes)
    • 1 1/2 cups unsweetened almond milk
    • 1/2 cup nutritional yeast
    • 2 tablespoons tahini (optional, for added creaminess)
    • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
    • 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
    • 2 tablespoons olive oil (or melted vegan butter)
    • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
    • 1 teaspoon onion powder
    • 1 teaspoon mustard (yellow or Dijon)
    • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric (for color, optional)
    • 1 teaspoon salt (or to taste)
    • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper (or to taste)
    • 1/4 teaspoon smoked paprika (optional, for added depth)
  • Optional Toppings
    • Freshly chopped parsley or chives
    • Paprika or smoked paprika (for garnish)
    • Breadcrumbs (toasted in olive oil, if desired, for crunch)
