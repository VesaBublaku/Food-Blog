Spaghetti with Crispy Zucchini

Servings: 2-5 servings
Prep Time : 20 minutes
Cook Time : 25 minutes
Total Time : 45 minutes

Start by slicing the zucchini into 1/4-inch thick rounds or half-moons, then coat each slice in a mixture of flour, garlic powder, onion powder, oregano, salt, and pepper, followed by dipping them into beaten egg and then pressing them into a breadcrumb-Parmesan mixture.

Heat 2 tablespoons of olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat and fry the zucchini slices in batches for 2-3 minutes per side, until golden and crispy. Remove the zucchini from the skillet and set them aside on a paper towel-lined plate.

While frying the zucchini, cook the spaghetti in a large pot of salted boiling water according to the package instructions, reserving about 1 cup of pasta water before draining. In a separate pan, heat 2 tablespoons of olive oil over medium heat and saut√© the minced garlic and red pepper flakes for about 1 minute until fragrant.

Add the drained spaghetti to the pan with the garlic, tossing to coat, and add some reserved pasta water if needed to loosen the sauce. Season with salt and pepper.

Gently fold the crispy zucchini into the pasta, reserving some for garnish. Serve the pasta topped with freshly grated Parmesan cheese, fresh basil or parsley, and a squeeze of lemon juice if desired.

Ingredients List

  • For the Crispy Zucchini
    • 2 medium zucchinis, sliced into 1/4-inch thick rounds
    • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
    • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
    • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
    • 1/4 teaspoon dried oregano
    • Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste
    • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs (preferably panko for extra crunch)
    • 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
    • 1 large egg, beaten
    • 2 tablespoons olive oil (for frying)
  • For the Pasta
    • 12 ounces spaghetti (or any pasta of your choice)
    • Salt (for pasta water)
    • 2 tablespoons olive oil
    • 2 cloves garlic, minced
    • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional, for heat)
    • Freshly ground black pepper, to taste
    • 1/2 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese
    • Fresh basil or parsley, chopped (for garnish)
    • Lemon wedges (optional, for serving)
