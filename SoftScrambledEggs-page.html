Soft Scrambled Eggs

Servings: 1-2
Prep Time : 1-2 minutes
Cook Time : 4-5 minutes
Total Time : 5-7 minutes

Crack the eggs into a bowl, add a pinch of salt, and whisk them together until the yolks and whites are fully combined. For extra creaminess, whisk in 2 tablespoons of heavy cream or milk.

Heat a non-stick skillet over low to medium-low heat and melt 2 tablespoons of unsalted butter, making sure it doesn’t brown. Once the butter is melted, pour the beaten eggs into the pan and let them sit undisturbed for about 30 seconds.

Then, gently stir the eggs from the edges toward the center using a spatula, forming large soft curds.

Continue to stir every 10-15 seconds for about 2-4 minutes, making sure the eggs cook slowly. Once they are soft and slightly runny, remove them from the heat, as they will continue to cook with residual heat.

Serve immediately, garnished with freshly ground black pepper, herbs, or cheese if desired.

Ingredients List

  • For the Scrambled Eggs
    • 4 large eggs (fresh, room temperature for best results)
    • 2 tbsp unsalted butter (for richness and creaminess)
    • 2 tbsp heavy cream or milk (optional, for extra creaminess)
    • Pinch of salt (to taste)
    • Freshly ground black pepper (to taste)
  • Optional Add-ins (for extra flavor)
    • Chopped fresh herbs (like chives, parsley, or dill)
    • Grated cheese (like cheddar, feta, or parmesan)
    • Crumbled cooked bacon or sausage (for savory flavor)
    • Sautéed vegetables (like mushrooms, spinach, or bell peppers)
