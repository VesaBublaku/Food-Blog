Turtle Caramel Cake

Servings: 6-8
Prep Time : 30-40 minutes
Cook Time : 25-30 minutes
Total Time : 2- 2 hour 30 minutes

Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, and cinnamon. In a large mixing bowl, beat the softened butter and sugar until light and fluffy, about 3-4 minutes, then add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition, and stir in the vanilla.

With the mixer on low, alternate adding the dry ingredients with the sour cream and buttermilk, mixing until just combined. Divide the batter between the prepared pans and bake for 25-30 minutes, or until a toothpick comes out clean.

Let the cakes cool in the pans for 10 minutes, then transfer them to wire racks to cool completely. To make the caramel filling, melt the butter in a saucepan, then stir in the brown sugar and cream, bringing it to a simmer and cooking for 5-7 minutes until slightly thickened.

Remove from heat and stir in vanilla and salt, then fold in the toasted pecans. For the frosting, melt the chocolate chips, cream, and butter in a small saucepan over medium-low heat until smooth, then stir in the powdered sugar. Once the cakes have cooled, spread half of the caramel-pecan mixture over the first cake layer, top with the second cake, and spread the remaining caramel mixture over the top.

Pour the chocolate frosting over the cake, allowing it to drip down the sides, and garnish with the remaining pecans and a drizzle of caramel sauce. Let the cake set for 15-20 minutes before serving.

Ingredients List

  • For the Cake
    • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
    • 2 teaspoon baking powder
    • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
    • 1/4 teaspoon salt
    • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
    • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
    • 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
    • 4 large eggs
    • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
    • 1 cup sour cream (room temperature)
    • 1/2 cup buttermilk (room temperature)
  • For the Caramel Filling
    • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
    • 1 cup packed light brown sugar
    • 1/2 cup heavy cream
    • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
    • 1/4 teaspoon salt
    • 1 1/2 cups chopped pecans, toasted
  • For the Frosting
    • 1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips or chocolate chunks
    • 1/2 cup heavy cream
    • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
    • 1/4 cup powdered sugar
  • For Garnish
    • 1/2 cup chopped pecans
    • 1/4 cup caramel sauce (store-bought or homemade)
