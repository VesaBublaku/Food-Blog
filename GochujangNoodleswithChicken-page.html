Gochujang Noodles with Chicken

Servings: 4 Servings (2 tacos per person)
Prep Time : 10 minutes
Cook Time : 15 - 20 minutes
Total Time : 25 - 30 minutes

To make Gochujang Noodles with Chicken, start by marinating 2 boneless chicken breasts in a mixture of 1 tbsp soy sauce, 1 tbsp sesame oil, 1 tsp garlic powder, 1 tsp grated ginger, 1 tbsp rice vinegar, salt, and pepper for at least 15 minutes.

Cook 200g of noodles according to package instructions, then drain and set aside, drizzling with sesame oil to prevent sticking. In a small bowl, whisk together 2 tbsp gochujang, 2 tbsp soy sauce, 1 tbsp rice vinegar, 1 tbsp sesame oil, 1 tsp sugar, 1 tbsp water, and 1 tsp minced garlic to make the sauce.

Heat a skillet with oil over medium-high heat and cook the marinated chicken for about 5-7 minutes per side, until fully cooked, then slice it into strips. In the same skillet, stir-fry 1/2 sliced onion, 1 julienned red bell pepper, and 1 sliced zucchini for 3-4 minutes, adding 1 tsp minced garlic and 1 tbsp grated ginger for the last minute. Add the cooked noodles to the skillet, pour over the gochujang sauce, and toss to coat everything evenly.

Serve the noodles topped with the sliced chicken and garnish with sesame seeds if desired.

Ingredients List

  • For the Chicken
    • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
    • 1 tbsp soy sauce
    • 1 tbsp sesame oil
    • 1 tsp garlic powder
    • 1 tsp ginger, grated
    • Salt and pepper, to taste
    • 1 tbsp rice vinegar
  • For the Noodles
    • 200g (about 7 oz) noodles
    • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
    • 1/2 onion
    • 1 red bell pepper
    • 1 zucchini, sliced
    • 2 garlic cloves
    • 1 tbsp ginger
  • For the Gochujang Sauce
    • 2 tbsp gochujang
    • 2 tbsp soy sauce
    • 1 tbsp rice vinegar
    • 1 tbsp sesame oil
    • 1 tsp sugar
    • 1 tbsp water
    • 1 tsp garlic,minced
    • 1/2 tsp sesame seeds
