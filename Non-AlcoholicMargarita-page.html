Non-Alcoholic Margarita

Servings: 2 - 3
Prep Time : 5 minutes

To make a non-alcoholic margarita, combine 1/2 cup of freshly squeezed lime juice, 1/4 cup of freshly squeezed orange juice, 2 tablespoons of agave syrup or simple syrup, and 1/4 cup of water or club soda in a shaker or blender.

Add ice and shake vigorously for 15 seconds or blend until smooth. Rim your glasses with lime juice and dip them into coarse salt or sugar

Pour the margarita into the glasses over fresh ice and garnish with lime wedges. Optionally, add a splash of cranberry juice for color or mint leaves for extra flavor.

Ingredients List

  • For the Margarita
    • 1/2 cup freshly squeezed lime juice
    • 1/4 cup freshly squeezed orange juice
    • 2 tablespoons agave syrup or simple syrup
    • 1/4 cup water or club soda
    • Ice cubes
    • Coarse salt or sugar
    • Lime wedges (for garnish)
