Egg and Croissant Brunch Bake

Servings: 3-5
Prep Time : 15 minutes
Cook Time : 35-45 minutes
Total Time : 1 hour

Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and butter a 9x13-inch baking dish. Tear the croissants into large chunks and place them in the prepared dish. In a large bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, heavy cream (if using), garlic powder, salt, and pepper, then stir in the shredded cheese and chopped green onions (reserve some for topping if desired).

If using bacon, sausage, or vegetables, stir them into the egg mixture. Pour the egg mixture evenly over the croissant chunks, ensuring all pieces are soaked, and gently press down on the croissants to help them absorb the mixture.

Let it sit for 10-15 minutes to soak. Bake in the preheated oven for 35-45 minutes or until the eggs are set and the top is golden and slightly crispy.

Let the bake cool for a few minutes before serving, and garnish with fresh herbs, extra cheese, or green onions if desired.

Optionally serve with hot sauce or ketchup.

Ingredients List

  • For the Bake
    • 4 large croissants (preferably day-old for better absorption)
    • 8 large eggs
    • 1 cup whole milk (or half-and-half for creamier texture)
    • 1 cup shredded cheese (cheddar, Gruyère)
    • 1/2 cup cooked bacon or sausage
    • 1/4 cup green onions or chives, chopped
    • 1/4 tsp garlic powder (optional, for extra flavor)
    • Salt and black pepper, to taste
    • 1 tbsp butter (for greasing the baking dish)
    • 1/4 cup heavy cream (optional, for extra richness)
  • Optional Toppings and Garnishes
    • Fresh herbs (parsley, chives, or thyme), for garnish
    • Hot sauce or ketchup, for serving (optional)
