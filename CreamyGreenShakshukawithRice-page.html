Creamy Green Shakshuka with Rice

Servings: 2-3
Prep Time : 10-15 minutes
Cook Time : 15-18 minutes
Total Time : 40-45 minutes

Rinse the basmati rice under cold water until the water runs clear, then bring 2 cups of water (or vegetable broth) to a boil in a medium pot, add the rice, a pinch of salt, and 1 tbsp of olive oil (optional), stir, cover, and simmer on low heat for 15-18 minutes, then remove from heat and let it sit, covered, for 5 minutes before fluffing with a fork.

While the rice cooks, heat 2 tbsp olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat, add the chopped onion, and cook for 5 minutes until softened, then add the minced garlic, diced zucchini, and green bell pepper, cooking for another 5 minutes until the vegetables soften.

Stir in the spinach, parsley, cilantro, and green chilies (if using), cooking for 2-3 minutes until the spinach wilts, then add cumin, turmeric, ground coriander, salt, and pepper, stirring well. Pour in the coconut milk and vegetable broth, stir to combine, and simmer for 5-7 minutes, allowing the sauce to thicken.

Make four wells in the sauce, crack an egg into each well, cover the skillet with a lid, and cook for 5-7 minutes, or until the egg whites are set but the yolks are still runny (or cook longer for firmer yolks).

To serve, spoon rice onto plates, top with the creamy green shakshuka, and garnish with crumbled feta cheese, fresh herbs, and a dollop of sour cream or yogurt if desired.

Ingredients List

  • For the Rice
    • 1 cup basmati rice (or any long-grain rice)
    • 2 cups water (or vegetable broth for more flavor)
    • 1 tbsp olive oil (optional)
    • 1/2 tsp salt
  • For the Shakshuka Sauce
    • 2 tbsp olive oil
    • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
    • 2 cloves garlic, minced
    • 1 medium zucchini, diced
    • 1 cup spinach (fresh or frozen)
    • 1/2 cup fresh parsley, chopped
    • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
    • 1/2 cup green bell pepper, diced
    • 1-2 green chilies (optional, for heat), chopped
    • 1/2 cup canned coconut milk (for creaminess)
    • 1/2 cup vegetable broth (or water)
    • 1 tsp ground cumin
    • 1/2 tsp ground turmeric
    • 1/2 tsp ground coriander
    • Salt and black pepper, to taste
  • Toppings (optional)
    • 1/4 cup feta cheese, crumbled (optional)
    • Fresh herbs (parsley or cilantro), for garnish
    • Sour cream or yogurt, for serving (optional)
