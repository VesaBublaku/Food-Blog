Cookie Dough Energy Bites

Servings: 4-5
Prep Time : 10 minutes
Chill Time : 30 minutes
Total Time : 40 minutes

In a large mixing bowl, combine the rolled oats, peanut butter, honey, ground flaxseed, vanilla extract, and salt, stirring until everything is well mixed.

Add in the mini chocolate chips, almond flour (or oat flour), and protein powder (if using), and continue to mix until evenly distributed, forming a thick dough.

If the mixture feels too dry, add a bit more honey or peanut butter until it holds together. Stir in any optional add-ins like coconut flakes, hemp seeds, or dried fruit if desired.

Scoop out tablespoon-sized portions and roll them into balls with your hands, making about 12-15 bites. Place the balls on a parchment-lined baking sheet and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to firm up.

Once chilled, store the energy bites in an airtight container in the fridge for up to one week or freeze for longer shelf life.

Ingredients List

  • For the Base
    • 1 cup old-fashioned rolled oats
    • 1/2 cup natural peanut butter
    • 1/4 cup honey (or maple syrup for a vegan option)
    • 1/4 cup ground flaxseed (for fiber and omega-3s)
    • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract (for flavor)
    • 1/4 tsp salt (optional, for balance)
  • For the Cookie Dough Flavor
    • 1/4 cup mini chocolate chips (or dark chocolate chips)
    • 1/4 cup almond flour (or oat flour for a gluten-free option)
    • 2 tbsp protein powder (vanilla flavor or unflavored)
    • tbsp chia seeds (optional, for added texture and nutrients)
  • Optional Add-ins
    • 1 tbsp coconut flakes (for texture and flavor)
    • 1 tbsp hemp seeds (for added protein and healthy fats)
    • 1/4 cup dried fruit (such as raisins, cranberries)
