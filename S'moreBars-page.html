S'more Bars

Servings: 9 servings
Prep Time : 15 minutes
Chilling Time : 2-3 hours
Total Time : 2-3 hours

In a large bowl, combine the graham cracker crumbs, granulated sugar, and salt, then stir in the melted butter until the mixture is evenly coated and the crumbs are moistened. Press the mixture into the bottom of a 9x9-inch or 9x13-inch baking pan lined with parchment paper or sprayed with cooking spray, then chill in the refrigerator for at least 15 minutes.

In a small saucepan, melt the milk chocolate chips and heavy cream over low heat, stirring until smooth, then remove from heat and stir in the 2 cups of mini marshmallows until they begin to soften but donâ€™t fully melt.

Pour the chocolate-marshmallow mixture over the chilled crust and spread it evenly. Sprinkle the remaining mini marshmallows over the top, then refrigerate the bars for 2 hours or until firm.

Optionally, melt the remaining 1/2 cup of chocolate chips and drizzle over the top of the bars, or toast the marshmallows under the broiler for 1-2 minutes for a caramelized effect.

Once set, cut the bars into squares and serve, storing any leftovers in the refrigerator for up to 5 days.

Ingredients List

  • For the Crust
    • 2 cups graham cracker crumbs (about 12 graham crackers crushed)
    • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
    • 1/2 teaspoon salt
    • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • For the Filling
    • 2 cups mini marshmallows (plus extra for topping)
    • 1 1/2 cups milk chocolate chips (or chopped milk chocolate)
    • 1/4 cup heavy cream
  • For the Topping
    • 1/2 cup mini marshmallows (for additional topping)
    • 1/2 cup milk chocolate chips (optional, for drizzling)
