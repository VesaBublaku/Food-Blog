Slow Cooked Shredded Beeragu Pasta

Servings: 5-7 servings
Prep Time : 10-15 minutes
Cook Time : 2-3 hours
Total Time : 3-4 gours

Heat olive oil in a large pot or Dutch oven over medium heat and sauté the chopped onion, carrots, and celery for 6–8 minutes until softened. Add minced garlic and cook for another minute, then add the ground beef (or beef and pork mixture), breaking it up as it browns for 8–10 minutes.

Pour in the red wine (if using) to deglaze the pot, scraping up any bits, and cook for 2 minutes. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, beef broth (or water), oregano, thyme, bay leaf, salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes (if using).

Bring to a simmer, then reduce heat to low and let the sauce cook for 2–3 hours, stirring occasionally. If using a slow cooker, transfer the mixture and cook on low for 4–6 hours.

Once done, remove the meat from the sauce, shred it with two forks, and return it to the pot, stirring in any additional cream or milk if desired. Meanwhile, cook the pasta in salted boiling water according to package instructions, reserving a cup of pasta water before draining.

Combine the pasta with the ragu, adding reserved pasta water if necessary, and serve with freshly grated Parmesan cheese and chopped fresh basil or parsley.

Ingredients List

  • For the Ragu (Bolognese Sauce)
    • 2 tablespoons olive oil
    • 1 onion, finely chopped
    • 2 medium carrots, peeled and finely chopped
    • 2 celery stalks, finely chopped
    • 4 cloves garlic, minced
    • 1 pound ground beef (or a mix of beef and pork for added richness)
    • 1/2 cup red wine (optional but recommended)
    • 1 can (14 oz) crushed tomatoes
    • 1 can (6 oz) tomato paste
    • 1/2 cup beef broth or water
    • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
    • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
    • 1 bay leaf
    • Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste
    • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional, for heat)
    • 1 tablespoon sugar (optional, to balance acidity)
    • 2 tablespoons heavy cream or whole milk (optional)
  • For the Pasta
    • 1 pound of pasta (tagliatelle, pappardelle, or fettuccine)
    • Salt for pasta water
  • Garnishes
    • Freshly grated Parmesan cheese
    • Fresh basil or parsley, chopped
