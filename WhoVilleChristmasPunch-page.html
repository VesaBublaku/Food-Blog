WhoVille Christmas Punch

Servings: 4 - 6
Prep Time : 5 - 7 minutes

To make the WhoVille Christmas Punch, combine 1 1/2 cups white rum, 1 1/2 cups cranberry juice, 1 cup pineapple juice, 1/2 cup orange juice, 1/4 cup lime juice, and 1/4 cup simple syrup in a large pitcher or bowl

Stir well to combine, then add a few slices of fresh ginger if desired. Fill glasses with ice, then pour the punch into each glass, topping with a splash of sparkling water or club soda for some fizz.

Garnish with fresh cranberries, orange slices, cinnamon sticks, and mint sprigs if you like. Serve and enjoy this festive, fruity punch!

Ingredients List

  • For The Punch
    • 1 1/2 cups White rum
    • 1 1/2 cups Cranberry juice
    • 1 cup Pineapple juice
    • 1/2 cup Orange juice
    • 1/4 cup Lime juice
    • 1/4 cup Simple syrup
    • 1-2 slices Fresh ginger
    • Sparkling water or club soda
    • Ice(for serving)
  • Garnish
    • Cinnamon stick
    • Fresh cranberries
    • Orange slices
    • Mint springs
