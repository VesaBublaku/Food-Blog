Shortcut Sesame Butter Noodles

Servings: 2
Prep Time : 5 minutes
Cook Time : 10 minutes
Total Time : 15 minutes

Start by cooking 8 oz of noodles according to package instructions until al dente, then drain and set aside, reserving a little pasta water.

While the noodles cook, make the sesame butter sauce by combining 2 tablespoons peanut butter or tahini, 1 1/2 tablespoons soy sauce, 1 tablespoon sesame oil, 1 tablespoon rice vinegar or lime juice, 1 tablespoon honey or maple syrup, 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder, and 1/2 teaspoon ginger powder in a bowl; whisk until smooth.

If the sauce seems too thick, add 1-2 tablespoons of warm water or reserved pasta water, a little at a time, until it reaches a creamy consistency that will easily coat the noodles. Add the cooked noodles to the bowl with the sauce, tossing well to ensure each noodle is coated.

Serve warm or chilled, topped with sliced green onions, sesame seeds, chili flakes, or a handful of chopped peanuts for added flavor and crunch.

Ingredients List

  • For the Noodle
    • 8 oz noodles
    • 2 tablespoons peanut butter or tahini
    • 1 1/2 tablespoons soy sauce
    • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
    • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar or lime juice
    • 1 tablespoon honey or maple syrup
    • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
    • 1/2 teaspoon ginger powder
    • Optional toppings: sliced green onions, sesame seeds, chili flakes
