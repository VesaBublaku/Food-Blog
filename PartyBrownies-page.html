Party Brownies

Servings: 2-5
Prep Time : 15 minutes
Cook Time : 25-30 minutes
Total Time : 1- 1 hour 15 minutes

Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a 9x13-inch baking pan or line it with parchment paper. In a large bowl, whisk together the melted butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until smooth, then add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition, followed by the vanilla extract.

In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, and salt, then gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture, stirring until well combined.

Fold in the chocolate chips, then pour the batter into the prepared pan, spreading it evenly. For extra flair, sprinkle mini marshmallows, crushed nuts, and extra chocolate chips on top.

Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out with a few moist crumbs (not wet batter).

Let the brownies cool completely in the pan on a wire rack before slicing them into squares.

Ingredients List

  • For the Brownies
    • 1 box of brownie mix (or homemade brownie mix, about 18 oz)
    • 2 large eggs
    • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
    • 1/4 cup water (or as per box instructions)
    • 1 cup mini chocolate chips (optional)
    • 1/2 cup candy (e.g., M&Ms, Skittles, or any colorful candy you like)
    • 1/4 cup chopped nuts
  • For Topping and Garnish
    • 1/4 cup caramel sauce (optional, but adds a great flavor twist)
    • 1/4 cup sprinkles (for color and fun)
    • 1/4 cup chocolate or vanilla frosting (optional for frosting on top)
    • 1/4 cup crushed cookies (Oreos, graham crackers, or any favorite cookie)
