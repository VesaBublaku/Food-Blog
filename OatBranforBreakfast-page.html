Oat Bran for Breakfast

Servings: 1-2
Prep Time : 1-2 minutes
Cook Time : 4-5 minutes
Total Time : 10 minutes

In a medium saucepan, combine 1/2 cup of oat bran with 1 1/2 cups of water (or milk for creaminess) and a pinch of salt, then bring the mixture to a simmer over medium heat, stirring frequently to prevent lumps.

Cook for about 4-5 minutes until the oat bran thickens and reaches a creamy consistency, stirring occasionally.

Once itâ€™s done, taste and adjust the consistency by adding more water or milk if needed. Remove from heat, then stir in honey or maple syrup for sweetness and ground cinnamon for flavor.

Spoon the oat bran into a bowl and top with your choice of fresh or dried fruit, nuts or seeds, and a dollop of nut butter.

Optionally, add Greek yogurt for extra creaminess, and serve immediately.

Ingredients List

  • For the Oat Bran
    • 1/2 cup oat bran
    • 1 1/2 cups water (or milk for a creamier texture)
    • 1/4 tsp salt (optional, to taste)
  • For Toppings (optional but recommended)
    • 1 tbsp honey or maple syrup (for sweetness)
    • 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon (for flavor)
    • 1/4 cup fresh or dried fruit (such as berries, banana slices)
    • 1/4 cup nuts or seeds (such as almonds, walnuts, chia seeds)
    • 1 tbsp nut butter (peanut butter, almond butter)
    • 1/4 cup Greek yogurt (optional, for creaminess and added protein)
