The Santa Clause Smash

Servings: 1
Prep Time : 5 minutes

In a cocktail shaker, muddle the sage leaves with simple syrup to release the herbal aroma. Add the bourbon (or rum), cranberry juice, and orange juice to the shaker along with ice

Shake well until chilled. Strain into a rocks glass filled with ice. Garnish with a few fresh cranberries and an orange wheel for a festive touch.

This drink is a perfect balance of fruity, sweet, and herbal flavors, making it an ideal choice for a holiday gathering or a cozy winter evening!

Ingredients List

  • Base Spirits
    • 2 oz bourbon
  • Fruit
    • 1 oz cranberry juice
    • 1 oz fresh orange juice
  • Herbs
    • 2-3 fresh sage leaves

  • Garnish
    • Cranberries and orange wheel
