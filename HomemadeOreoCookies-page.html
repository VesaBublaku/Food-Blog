Homemade Oreo Cookies

Servings: 3-6
Prep Time : 10 minutes
Cook Time : 20 minutes
Total Time : 1 hour 25 minutes

To make homemade Oreo cookies, start by whisking together flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, and salt in a large bowl; in another bowl, beat softened butter and sugar until light and fluffy (about 2-3 minutes), then add the egg and vanilla extract, mixing until smooth.

Gradually mix the dry ingredients into the wet ingredients until a dough forms, then divide the dough in half, wrap each portion in plastic wrap, and refrigerate for 30 minutes to firm up. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and line baking sheets with parchment paper.

On a lightly floured surface, roll out the dough to about 1/8-inch (0.3 cm) thickness, then use a round cookie cutter (about 1.5 inches or 4 cm in diameter) to cut out circles.

Place the circles on prepared baking sheets, spacing them about 1 inch apart, and bake for 10-12 minutes. Let the cookies cool completely on a wire rack. For the filling, beat the softened butter until smooth, gradually adding powdered sugar, vanilla extract, and a pinch of salt, mixing until creamy.

To assemble, spread or pipe a small amount of filling onto the flat side of one cookie, then top with a second cookie, pressing gently to sandwich them together.

Ingredients List

  • Chocolate Wafer Ingredients
    • 190 grams (1 1/2 cups) all-purpose flour
    • 60 grams (3/4 cup) Dutch-process cocoa powder
    • 200 grams (1 cup) granulated sugar
    • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
    • 1/2 teaspoon salt
    • 150 grams (2/3 cup) unsalted butter, softened
    • 1 large egg, at room temperature
    • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Cream Filling Ingredients
    • 110 grams (1/2 cup) unsalted butter, softened
    • Powdered Sugar: 180 grams (1 1/2 cups), sifted
    • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
    • A pinch of salt
    • 100 grams almond paste or marzipan
