Mucci's Bucatini

Servings: 4 servings
Prep Time : 10 minutes
Cook Time : 20 minutes
Total Time : 30 minutes

Cook the bucatini pasta in a large pot of salted boiling water according to the package instructions, reserving 1 cup of pasta water before draining.

In a skillet, heat 2 tbsp olive oil over medium heat, then cook 4 oz diced pancetta until crispy, about 4-5 minutes. Add 4 minced garlic cloves and 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes, cooking for 1 minute until fragrant.

Pour in 1/2 cup dry white wine and let it cook off for 2 minutes. Add 1 (14.5 oz) can of crushed tomatoes and simmer for 10 minutes. Stir in 1/4 cup heavy cream and cook for another 2 minutes, seasoning with salt and pepper to taste. Toss the drained bucatini into the sauce, adding reserved pasta water if needed to reach the desired sauce consistency.

Stir in 1/2 cup grated Pecorino Romano cheese, and garnish with fresh basil leaves before serving.

Ingredients List

  • For the Pasta
    • 1 lb bucatini pasta
    • 2 tbsp olive oil
    • 4 oz pancetta, diced
    • 4 garlic cloves, minced
    • 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
    • 1/2 cup dry white wine
    • 1 (14.5 oz) can of crushed tomatoes
    • 1/4 cup heavy cream
    • 1/2 cup grated Pecorino Romano cheese
    • Fresh basil leaves
    • Salt and pepper to taste
