One-Pot Spicy Eggs and Potatoes

Servings: 4-5
Prep Time : 10 minutes
Cook Time : 25-30 minutes
Total Time : 40-45 minutes

In a large skillet or nonstick pan, heat olive oil over medium heat, add cumin seeds, and let them sizzle for about 30 seconds until fragrant. Add chopped onion and saut√© until golden and soft, about 5-7 minutes, then add minced garlic and cook for another minute.

Stir in diced bell pepper, turmeric, smoked paprika, ground cumin, ground coriander, chili flakes, salt, and pepper, and cook for about 2 minutes to soften the bell pepper. Add diced potatoes, tossing to coat them in the spiced mixture.

Cover the pan and cook for about 10-12 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the potatoes are tender. Add a splash of water if the pan becomes too dry. Stir in diced tomatoes and cook for another 3-5 minutes until softened.

Using a spoon, create four small wells in the potato mixture and crack an egg into each well. Reduce heat to low, cover the pan, and cook until the eggs are done to your liking (6-7 minutes for firm yolks, 4-5 minutes for soft yolks).

If extra steam is needed, add a bit of water around the pan's edges. Once the eggs are done, remove from heat and garnish with fresh cilantro or parsley before serving.

Ingredients List

  • Vegetables
    • 4 medium potatoes, peeled and diced into 1-inch cubes
    • 1 large onion, finely chopped
    • 1 bell pepper (any color), diced
    • 2-3 cloves garlic, minced
    • 1 medium tomato, diced
  • Protein
    • 4 large eggs
  • Spices and Seasoning
    • 1 tsp cumin seeds
    • 1 tsp smoked paprika (or regular paprika if unavailable)
    • 1/2 tsp ground turmeric
    • 1/2 tsp ground cumin
    • 1/2 tsp ground coriander
    • 1/2-1 tsp chili flakes (adjust to taste)
    • Salt and black pepper, to taste
  • Fresh Herbs
    • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro or parsley, chopped (for garnish)
  • Oils and Liquids
    • 2-3 tbsp olive oil (or vegetable oil)
    • 1/4 cup water (optional, for steaming eggs if needed)
