Korean BBQ Burrito

Servings: 4-6
Prep Time : 30 minutes
Cook Time : 25 minutes
Total Time : 55 minutes

To make the Korean BBQ Burrito, start by marinating the thinly sliced beef in a mixture of soy sauce, sesame oil, brown sugar, minced garlic, grated ginger, rice vinegar, gochujang (if using), and mirin for at least 30 minutes, ideally 2 hours

While the beef marinates, cook the rice according to package instructions, then mix it with rice vinegar, sugar, and a pinch of salt while it's still warm. Prepare the vegetables by chopping the kimchi, julienning the cucumber, carrot, and red bell pepper, and chopping the cilantro and green onions.

In a large skillet or grill pan, cook the marinated beef over medium-high heat for 5-7 minutes, until it's cooked through and caramelized

To make the spicy mayo, whisk together mayonnaise, gochujang, rice vinegar, and honey until smooth. To assemble the burritos, warm the flour tortillas and layer each with a scoop of rice, cooked beef, kimchi, cucumber, carrot, red bell pepper, cilantro, green onions, and a drizzle of spicy mayo

Fold the sides of the tortilla and roll it tightly to secure the filling. Serve the burritos warm, cut in half, and garnish with extra cilantro, green onions, or sesame seeds if desired. Enjoy your delicious Korean BBQ Burritos

Ingredients List

  • For the Korean BBQ Beef
    • 1 lb (450g) beef sirloin or rib-eye, thinly sliced
    • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
    • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
    • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
    • 2 cloves garlic, minced
    • 1 tablespoon ginger
    • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
    • 1 tablespoon gochujang
    • 1 tablespoon mirin
    • 2 teaspoons sesame seeds
    • 1 tablespoon green onion, chopped (for garnish)
  • For the Rice
    • 2 cups cooked jasmine or short-grain rice
    • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
    • 1 teaspoon sugar
    • Pinch of salt
  • For the Vegetables
    • 1 cup kimchi, chopped
    • 1 medium cucumber
    • 1 small carrot
    • 1 small red bell peppe
    • 1/4 cup cilantro leaves
  • For the Burrito Wrap
    • 4 large flour tortillas
  • For the Spicy Mayo Sauce
    • 3 tablespoons mayonnaise
    • 1 tablespoon gochujang
    • 1 teaspoon rice vinegar
    • 1 teaspoon honey or sugar
