Spicy Creamy Coconut Lime Margaritas

Servings: 1
Prep Time : 5 - 7 minutes

To make the Spicy Creamy Coconut Lime Margarita, start by preparing the rim of your glass by rubbing a lime wedge along the edge and dipping it into a mixture of flaky sea salt, lime zest, and chili powder

In a cocktail shaker, combine 2 oz tequila, 1 oz triple sec, 1 oz fresh lime juice, 1 oz coconut milk, 1/2 oz agave syrup, and 1-2 slices of jalapeño

Add ice and shake vigorously for about 15-20 seconds. Fill your prepared glass with ice (optional), then strain the margarita mixture into the glass. Garnish with a lime wedge, fresh cilantro leaves, and a jalapeño slice. Enjoy the creamy, spicy, and tangy margarita!

Ingredients List

  • For The Margarita
    • 2 oz Tequila
    • 1 oz Triple Sec
    • 1 oz Fresh lime juice
    • 1 oz Coconut milk
    • 1/2 oz Agave syrup or simple syrup
    • 1-2 slices Jalapeño
    • Ice (for shaking)
  • For the Rim
    • 1/4 cup Flaky sea salt
    • 1 tbsp Lime zest
    • 1 tsp Chili powder or Tajín
  • Garnish
    • Lime wedges
    • Fresh cilantro leaves
    • 1 small carrot
