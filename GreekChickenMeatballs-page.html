Greek Chicken Meatballs

Servings: 2-6
Prep Time : 20-25 minutes
Cook Time : 18-20 minutes
Total Time : 35-40 minutes

Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper or lightly grease it. In a large mixing bowl, combine the ground chicken, breadcrumbs, egg, minced red onion, garlic, parsley, dill, oregano, cumin, salt, and black pepper, mixing gently until well combined.

Form the mixture into 1-inch meatballs using a tablespoon or cookie scoop and place them on the prepared baking sheet. Lightly brush or drizzle the meatballs with olive oil, then bake for 18-20 minutes, or until golden and cooked through (internal temperature should reach 165°F/74°C).

While the meatballs bake, make the tzatziki sauce by combining Greek yogurt, grated cucumber (squeezing out excess water), lemon juice, dill, garlic, olive oil, salt, and black pepper in a medium bowl and stirring until well mixed.

Chill the sauce in the fridge until ready to serve. Once the meatballs are done, serve them warm with the tzatziki sauce on the side, garnished with fresh parsley or dill, lemon wedges, and optional crumbled feta or sliced olives.

Enjoy with pita bread, a side salad, or as part of a mezze platter!

Ingredients List

  • Meatball Ingredients
    • Ground chicken – 1 lb (preferably lean)
    • Breadcrumbs – 1/2 cup (preferably panko for a light texture)
    • Egg – 1 large (for binding)
    • Red onion – 1/4 cup, finely minced
    • Garlic – 2 cloves, minced
    • Fresh parsley – 2 tablespoons, chopped
    • Fresh dill – 1 tablespoon, chopped
    • Dried oregano – 1 teaspoon
    • Ground cumin – 1/2 teaspoon
    • Salt – 1/2 teaspoon
    • Black pepper – 1/4 teaspoon
    • Olive oil – 1 tablespoon (for brushing or drizzling before baking)
  • Tzatziki Sauce
    • Greek yogurt – 1 cup (plain, full-fat for creaminess)
    • Cucumber – 1/2 cup, grated and drained of excess water
    • Lemon juice – 1 tablespoon (freshly squeezed)
    • Fresh dill – 1 tablespoon, finely chopped
    • Garlic – 1 clove, minced
    • Olive oil – 1 teaspoon
    • Salt – 1/4 teaspoon
    • Black pepper – a pinch
  • Optional Garnishes
    • Fresh parsley or dill – for garnish
    • Lemon wedges – for serving
    • Crumbled feta – 1/4 cup (for extra flavor)
    • Olives – 1/4 cup, sliced
