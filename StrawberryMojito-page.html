Strawberry Mojito

Servings: 1
Prep Time : 5 minutes

In a glass, muddle the strawberries, mint leaves, and lime wedges together until the strawberries are juicy and the mint is fragrant.

Add 1-2 tbsp simple syrup for sweetness, then pour in 2 oz of white rum.

Fill the glass with ice, top with club soda, and stir gently to combine. Garnish with a fresh strawberry slice and a sprig of mint. Enjoy this refreshing, fruity twist on the classic mojito!

Ingredients List

  • For The Mojito
    • 6-8 Fresh strawberries
    • 8-10 Fresh mint leaves
    • 1/2 Lime
    • 1-2 tbsp Simple syrup
    • 2 oz White rum
    • Club soda
    • Ice (for serving)
  • Garnish
    • Strawberry slice
    • Mint sprig
