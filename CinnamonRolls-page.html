Cinnamon Rolls

Servings: 4-6
Prep Time : 20-30 minutes
Cook Time : 25-30 minutes
Total Time : 3 hours

In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, sugar, yeast, and salt. In a small saucepan, warm the milk to about 110째F (43째C) and pour it into the flour mixture, followed by the melted butter, eggs, and vanilla extract, then stir to form a dough.

Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface and knead for about 5-7 minutes until smooth and elastic. Place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover it, and let it rise in a warm place for 1 hour, or until doubled in size.

Gradually add the dry ingredients to the butter mixture, alternating with the milk, beginning and ending with the dry ingredients, mixing just until combined. Fold in the chopped pistachios until evenly distributedFor the filling, mix together the softened butter, brown sugar, cinnamon, and salt until combined. Once the dough has risen, punch it down and roll it out into a 16x12-inch rectangle. Spread the cinnamon filling evenly over the dough, then roll it up tightly from one long edge and pinch the seam to seal.

Slice the roll into 12 pieces and place them in a greased 9x13-inch baking dish. Cover with a clean towel and let them rise for 30-45 minutes, until puffed. Preheat the oven to 350째F (175째C) and bake the rolls for 25-30 minutes until golden brown.

While the rolls are baking, whisk together the powdered sugar, milk, and vanilla extract for the glaze. Once the rolls are done, let them cool for 5 minutes, then drizzle with the glaze and serve warm. Enjoy!

Ingredients List

  • For the Dough
    • 4 cups all-purpose flour, plus extra for dusting
    • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
    • 2 teaspoons instant dry yeast
    • 1 teaspoon salt
    • 1 cup whole milk
    • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
    • 2 large eggs, at room temperature
    • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • For the Filling
    • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
    • 3/4 cup brown sugar, packed
    • 2 tablespoons ground cinnamon
    • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • For the Glaze
    • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
    • 2 tablespoons milk (or water, to thin)
    • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
    • Pinch of salt (optional)
