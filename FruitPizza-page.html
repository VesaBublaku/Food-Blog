Fruit Pizza

Servings: 6 - 8 servings
Prep Time : 15 minutes
Cook Time : 35 - 40 minutes
Total Time : 50 - 55 minutes

To make a refreshing fruit pizza, start by preheating your oven to 350°F (175°C).

Roll out 1 package of refrigerated sugar cookie dough onto a greased or parchment-lined baking sheet, forming a circular pizza crust shape. Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until golden brown. Once baked, remove from the oven and allow the crust to cool completely.

For the topping, beat 8 oz cream cheese with 1/4 cup powdered sugar and 1 teaspoon vanilla extract until smooth and creamy. Spread the cream cheese mixture evenly over the cooled cookie crust.

Arrange an assortment of fresh fruits on top, such as sliced strawberries, kiwi, blueberries, pineapple, and mandarin oranges. You can also add other fruits of your choice, like grapes or raspberries.

For a glossy finish, brush the top with a simple glaze made by heating 2 tablespoons of apricot or peach jam in a small pan until melted. Drizzle the glaze over the fruit to give it a shiny, sweet finish. Chill the fruit pizza in the fridge for 1 hour before slicing and serving.

Ingredients List

  • For the Pizza
    • 1 package refrigerated sugar cookie dough
    • 8 oz cream cheese, softened
    • 1/4 cup powdered sugar
    • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
    • Assorted fresh fruits (e.g., strawberries, kiwi, blueberries etc)
    • 2 tablespoons apricot or peach jam (for glaze)
