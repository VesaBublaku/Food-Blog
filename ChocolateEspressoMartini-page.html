Chocolate Espresso Martini

Servings: 1
Prep Time : 5 minutes

To make the Chocolate Espresso Martini, combine 1 oz vodka, 1 oz coffee liqueur, 1 oz cooled fresh brewed espresso, and 1/2 oz chocolate liqueur in a cocktail shaker.

Add ice and shake vigorously for about 15-20 seconds to chill and blend the flavors. Strain the mixture into a chilled martini glass

Garnish by dusting the top with cocoa powder and adding chocolate shavings or curls for an extra indulgent touch. Enjoy this rich, creamy, and energizing dessert cocktail!

Ingredients List

  • For The Drink
    • 1 oz Vodka
    • 1 oz Coffee liqueur
    • 11 oz Fresh brewed espresso
    • 1/2 oz Chocolate liqueur
    • 1/2 oz Simple syrup
    • Ice (for shaking)
  • Garnish
    • Cocoa powder
    • Chocolate shavings or chocolate curls
