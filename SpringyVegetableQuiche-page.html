Springy Vegetable Quiche

Servings: 4-7
Prep Time : 10-15 minutes
Cook Time : 35-40 minutes
Total Time : 1-1.5 hours

To make the Springy Vegetable Quiche, start by making the crust: in a food processor, pulse together the flour and salt, then add the cubed cold butter and pulse until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs. Gradually add ice water, one tablespoon at a time, until the dough begins to come together.

Form the dough into a disc, wrap it in plastic wrap, and chill in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes. While the dough chills, heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat, then sauté the onion and garlic until softened, about 2-3 minutes.

Add the diced red bell pepper and zucchini, cooking until tender for about 5-7 minutes, then stir in the spinach and cook until wilted. Remove from heat, add fresh herbs and black pepper, and let the vegetable mixture cool.

Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C), and roll out the chilled dough on a floured surface to fit a 9-inch tart pan or pie dish, pressing it into the edges and trimming any excess. Prick the bottom of the dough with a fork and bake for 10-12 minutes until lightly golden. While the crust cools, whisk together the eggs, heavy cream, milk, nutmeg, salt, and pepper in a bowl, then stir in the grated cheese.

Spread the cooled vegetable mixture into the baked crust, pour the egg custard over it, and bake for 35-40 minutes until the custard is set and lightly golden on top. Let the quiche cool for 5-10 minutes before slicing and serving, garnished with fresh herbs and extra cherry tomatoes if desired.

Ingredients List

  • For the Crust
    • All-purpose flour – 1 1/4 cups
    • Cold unsalted butter – 1/2 cup (cubed)
    • Salt – 1/2 teaspoon
    • Ice water – 3-4 tablespoons (chilled)
  • For the Filling
    • Olive oil – 1 tablespoon
    • Onion – 1 small (finely chopped)
    • Garlic – 2 cloves (minced)
    • Red bell pepper – 1 (diced)
    • Zucchini – 1 small (diced)
    • Fresh spinach – 2 cups (chopped)
    • Cherry tomatoes – 1/2 cup (halved)
    • Fresh herbs – 1 tablespoon (such as thyme, basil, or parsley, chopped)
    • Salt – 1/2 teaspoon
    • Black pepper – 1/4 teaspoon
  • For the Quiche Custard
    • Eggs – 4 large
    • Heavy cream – 1 cup (or use half-and-half for a lighter version)
    • Milk – 1/2 cup
    • Ground nutmeg – a pinch (optional, for extra flavor)
    • Cheese – 1 1/2 cups (grated; such as cheddar, Gruyère, or a mix of both)
  • Optional Garnish
    • Fresh herbs – extra thyme or parsley for garnish
    • Cherry tomatoes – a few extra halved for garnish
