Spicy Sofritas Tofu

Servings: 4 servings
Prep Time : 10 minutes
Cook Time : 15 - 20 minutes
Total Time : 25 - 30 minutes

To make spicy sofritas tofu, start by pressing 1 block of firm tofu to remove excess moisture, then crumble it into small pieces.

Heat 2 tablespoons of olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat, and saut√© the tofu for about 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally, until golden and crispy. While the tofu cooks, prepare the sofritas sauce by blending 1/4 cup tomato paste, 2 tablespoons soy sauce, 1 tablespoon chipotle peppers in adobo sauce (with some sauce for extra spice), 1 teaspoon smoked paprika, 1 teaspoon ground cumin, 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder, 1/2 teaspoon onion powder, and 1/2 cup vegetable broth in a blender or food processor until smooth.

Once the tofu is crispy, pour the sofritas sauce over it, and stir to combine. Let the tofu simmer in the sauce for about 5-10 minutes, allowing it to absorb the flavors. Adjust the seasoning with salt and pepper to taste.

Serve your spicy sofritas tofu over rice, in tacos, or as a filling for burritos.

Ingredients List

  • For the Sofritas
    • 1 block firm tofu
    • 2 tablespoons olive oil
    • 1/4 cup tomato paste
    • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
    • 1 tablespoon chipotle peppers in adobo sauce
    • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
    • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
    • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
    • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
    • 1/2 cup vegetable broth
    • Salt and pepper
