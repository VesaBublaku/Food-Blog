Peanut Butter Pie

Servings: 3-7
Prep Time : 20-30 minutes
Cook Time : 8-10 minutes
Total Time : 4-5 hours

Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a 9-inch pie dish. To make the crust, combine graham cracker crumbs, granulated sugar, and cinnamon in a bowl, then add melted butter and mix until the crumbs are coated.

Press the mixture into the pie dish and bake for 8-10 minutes until golden, then let it cool completely. For the filling, beat the peanut butter and cream cheese together until smooth, then add powdered sugar and vanilla extract and mix until combined.

In a separate bowl, whisk the heavy cream and sour cream until thickened, then fold it into the peanut butter mixture until smooth. Once the crust is cool, spread the peanut butter filling evenly into the crust and refrigerate for 3-4 hours or overnight to set.

To make the whipped topping, beat the heavy whipping cream, powdered sugar, and vanilla extract until stiff peaks form, then spread it over the chilled peanut butter filling.

For garnish, drizzle warmed peanut butter over the topping, and optionally add chocolate chips, hot fudge sauce, or chopped peanuts. Chill the pie until ready to serve and enjoy!

Ingredients List

  • For the Crust
    • 1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs (about 10-12 graham crackers)
    • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
    • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon (optional, for a hint of spice)
    • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • For the Peanut Butter Filling
    • 1 cup creamy peanut butter (preferably a good quality peanut butter)
    • 8 oz cream cheese, softened
    • 1 cup powdered sugar
    • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
    • 1/2 cup heavy whipping cream
    • 1/2 cup sour cream or Greek yogurt (for extra creaminess)
  • For the Whipped Topping
    • 1 cup heavy whipping cream
    • 2 tablespoons powdered sugar
    • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • For Garnish
    • 1/4 cup peanut butter (for drizzling on top)
    • 1/4 cup chocolate chips or hot fudge sauce (optional)
    • Chopped peanuts (optional)
