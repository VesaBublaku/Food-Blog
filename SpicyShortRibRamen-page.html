Spicy Short Rib Ramen

Servings: 4 servings
Prep Time : 15 minutes
Cook Time : 3 hours
Total Time : 3 hours 15 minutes

To make Spicy Short Rib Ramen, season 2 lbs of bone-in short ribs with salt and pepper, then brown them in 1 tbsp vegetable oil in a large oven-safe pot over medium-high heat.

Once browned, add 4 cups beef broth, 2 cups water, 1 quartered onion, 3 smashed garlic cloves, a sliced 2-inch piece of ginger, 1 tbsp soy sauce, and 1 tbsp miso paste (optional), bring to a boil, cover, and transfer to a 350Â°F oven for 2.5-3 hours until the ribs are tender.

For the broth, heat 2 tbsp vegetable oil and 1 tbsp sesame oil in a separate pot, cook 3 minced garlic cloves and 1-inch minced ginger for 1-2 minutes, then stir in 2 tbsp gochujang, 2 tbsp soy sauce, 1 tbsp rice vinegar, and 1 tbsp sugar.

Add 4 cups beef broth (or broth from the short ribs) and 2 cups water, simmer for 10 minutes.

Cook ramen noodles according to package instructions, then divide the noodles into bowls, pour the spicy broth over, and top with shredded short rib meat, soft-boiled eggs, sliced mushrooms, baby spinach, green onions, chili flakes, and sesame seeds before serving.

Ingredients List

  • For the Short Ribs
    • 2 lbs bone-in short ribs
    • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
    • Salt and pepper to taste
    • 4 cups beef broth
    • 2 cups water
    • 1 onion, quartered
    • 3 garlic cloves, smashed
    • 2-inch piece of ginger, sliced
    • 1 tbsp miso paste
  • For the Broth
    • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
    • 1 tbsp sesame oil
    • 3 garlic cloves, minced
    • 1-inch piece of ginger, minced
    • 2 tbsp gochujang
    • 2 tbsp soy sauce
    • 1 tbsp rice vinegar
    • 1 tbsp sugar
    • 4 cups beef broth
    • 2 cups water
  • For the Ramen
    • 4 servings of ramen noodles
    • 2 soft-boiled eggs (optional)
    • 1/2 cup green onions, sliced
    • 1/2 cup mushrooms
    • 1/2 cup baby spinach or bok choy
    • 1/2 tsp chili flakes
    • Sesame seeds
