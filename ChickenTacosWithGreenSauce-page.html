Chicken Tacos With Green Sauce

Servings: 4 Servings (2 tacos per person)
Prep Time : 10 minutes
Cook Time : 15 - 20 minutes
Total Time : 25 - 30 minutes

To begin, prepare the chicken by heating 1 tablespoon of olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. While the oil heats up, season 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts with 1 teaspoon of ground cumin, 1 teaspoon of garlic powder, 1/2 teaspoon of paprika, and salt and pepper to taste.

Once the oil is hot, add the chicken breasts to the pan and cook for 6-7 minutes on each side, or until they are golden brown and fully cooked through. You can check the internal temperature of the chicken with a meat thermometer; it should read 165°F (75°C) when done.

Once cooked, remove the chicken from the skillet and set it aside to rest for a few minutes. After resting, slice the chicken into thin strips or shred it using two forks, depending on your preference.

While the chicken is cooking, you can prepare the green sauce. In a blender or food processor, combine 2 husked and quartered tomatillos, 1-2 jalapeños (adjust to your desired spice level), 1/2 cup of fresh cilantro (leaves and stems), 1/4 cup of chopped onion, the juice of 1/2 lime, and 1/4 cup of water. Blend everything until the sauce is smooth. Taste the sauce and adjust the seasoning with salt as needed. If the sauce is too thick, you can add a little more water to reach your desired consistency.

Once the chicken and sauce are ready, warm 8 small tortillas (corn or flour, depending on your preference) in a dry skillet over medium heat or in the microwave for 15-20 seconds, just until they are soft and pliable.

To assemble the tacos, place a few slices or shreds of chicken in the center of each tortilla. Drizzle a generous amount of the green sauce over the chicken. For added flavor and color, garnish the tacos with a handful of freshly chopped cilantro, and if you like, sprinkle on some diced red onion or add slices of avocado for extra creaminess. You can also serve the tacos with lime wedges on the side for an extra burst of freshness.

Serve the tacos immediately while the tortillas are warm and the chicken is juicy. Enjoy these delicious chicken tacos with a zesty green sauce that will add a burst of flavor to your meal!

Ingredients List

  • For the Chicken
    • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
    • 1 tbsp olive oil
    • 1 tsp ground cumin
    • 1 tsp garlic powder
    • 1/2 tsp paprika
    • Salt and pepper, to taste
    • 1/2 lime
  • For the Green Sauce
    • 2 medium tomatillos, husked and quartered
    • 1-2 jalapeños
    • 1/2 cup cilantro
    • 1/4 cup onion, chopped
    • 1/2 lime
    • 1/4 cup water
    • Salt to taste
  • For the Tacos
    • 8 small soft corn or flour tortillas
    • Fresh cilantro
    • Diced red onion
    • Lime wedges
    • Avocado slices
