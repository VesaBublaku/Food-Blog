Spinach and Artichoke Pizza

Servings: 1-2
Prep Time : 20-30 minutes
Cook Time : 12-15 minutes
Total Time : 1-1.5 hours

To make the spinach and artichoke pizza, start by dissolving sugar and yeast in warm water and letting it sit for 5 minutes until frothy. In a large bowl, combine flour and salt, then add the yeast mixture and olive oil, stirring until a dough forms.

Knead the dough on a floured surface for 5-7 minutes until smooth, then place it in an oiled bowl, cover with a cloth, and let rise for about 1 hour. While the dough rises, heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat, add minced garlic, and cook for 1-2 minutes.

Add the thawed spinach and sauté for 3-4 minutes, seasoning with salt, pepper, and nutmeg. In a separate bowl, mix cream cheese, mozzarella, and Parmesan until smooth, then stir in the spinach and artichokes.

Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C) and line a baking sheet or pizza stone with parchment paper. Punch down the risen dough, roll it out to about 12 inches, and transfer it to the prepared baking sheet or stone.

Brush the dough with olive oil, spread the spinach and artichoke mixture evenly, and top with shredded mozzarella. Bake for 12-15 minutes until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly. Remove from the oven, let cool slightly, and garnish with fresh basil before serving.

Ingredients List

  • For the Pizza Dough
    • All-purpose flour – 2 1/2 cups
    • Active dry yeast – 1 teaspoon
    • Warm water – 1 cup (about 110°F / 43°C)
    • Olive oil – 2 tablespoons
    • Sugar – 1 teaspoon
    • Salt – 1 teaspoon
  • For the Spinach and Artichoke Topping
    • Frozen spinach – 1 1/2 cups (thawed and squeezed dry)
    • Canned artichoke hearts – 1 can, drained and chopped
    • Cream cheese – 4 oz (softened)
    • Mozzarella cheese – 1 1/2 cups (shredded)
    • Parmesan cheese – 1/2 cup (grated)
    • Garlic – 2 cloves (minced)
    • Olive oil – 1 tablespoon (for sautéing)
    • Salt – 1/2 teaspoon
    • Black pepper – 1/4 teaspoon
    • Nutmeg – 1/4 teaspoon (optional, for a subtle spice)
    • Lemon juice – 1 teaspoon (optional, to enhance flavor)
  • For Assembling the Pizza
    • Olive oil – 1 tablespoon (for brushing the crust)
    • Mozzarella cheese – 1 cup (shredded, for topping)
    • Fresh basil – 1/4 cup (chopped, for garnish)
