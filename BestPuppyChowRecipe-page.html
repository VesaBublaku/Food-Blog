Best Puppy Chow Recipe

Servings: 5-8
Prep Time : 10-15 minutes
Cooling Time : 10-15 minutes
Total Time : 20-25 minutes

Pour the Rice Chex cereal into a large mixing bowl and set aside. In a microwave-safe bowl, combine the chocolate chips, peanut butter, and butter, and microwave in 30-second intervals, stirring after each interval until smooth and fully melted (about 1-1.5 minutes total).

Stir in the vanilla extract, then pour the melted chocolate mixture over the cereal. Gently fold with a spatula until all the cereal is evenly coated, being careful not to crush it.

Transfer the coated cereal to a large resealable plastic bag, add the powdered sugar, seal the bag, and shake until the cereal is fully coated.

Spread the coated cereal on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper to cool and dry for about 10-15 minutes.

Once cooled, store in an airtight container or resealable bag and enjoy!

Ingredients List

  • For Puppy Chow
    • 9 cups Rice Chex cereal (or Corn Chex cereal)
    • 1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
    • 1/2 cup creamy peanut butter
    • 1/4 cup unsalted butter
    • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
    • 1 1/2 cups powdered sugar
