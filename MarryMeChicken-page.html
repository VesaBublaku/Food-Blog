Marry Me Chicken

Servings: 4
Prep Time : 10 minutes
Cook Time : 20 minutes
Total Time : 30 minutes

Season 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts generously with salt and pepper on both sides. In a large skillet over medium-high heat, warm 2 tablespoons olive oil and 1 tablespoon butter until melted and hot.

Carefully place the chicken breasts into the skillet, making sure they’re not overcrowded, and cook for 6-7 minutes on each side, allowing each side to become golden brown and develop a slight crust; the chicken should be cooked through to an internal temperature of 165°F. Once cooked, transfer the chicken to a plate and cover with foil to keep warm. In the same skillet, add 1/2 cup of chopped sun-dried tomatoes and 3 minced garlic cloves, stirring and sautéing for 1-2 minutes until fragrant, which allows the oil to infuse with the tomato and garlic flavors.

Pour in 1 cup heavy cream and 1/2 cup chicken broth, stirring well to deglaze the skillet and loosen any browned bits from cooking the chicken; this will add flavor to the sauce. Add 1/4 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese, 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning, and, if desired, 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes for a mild heat, then stir to combine.

Bring the sauce to a gentle simmer and cook for about 3-4 minutes, allowing it to thicken slightly while stirring occasionally. Reduce the heat to low, return the chicken breasts to the skillet, and spoon some sauce over each piece, allowing the chicken to warm and absorb some of the sauce flavors for about 5 minutes.

Remove from heat, garnish with freshly chopped basil, and serve immediately, spooning extra sauce over each chicken breast for added flavor and richness.

Ingredients List

  • For the Chicken
    • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
    • Salt and pepper, to taste
    • 2 tablespoons olive oil
    • 1 tablespoon butter
  • For the Sauce
    • 1/2 cup sun-dried tomatoes, drained and chopped
    • 3 cloves garlic, minced
    • 1 cup heavy cream
    • 1/2 cup chicken broth
    • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
    • 1 teaspoon dried Italian seasoning
    • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes
    • Fresh basil, for garnish
