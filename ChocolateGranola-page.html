Chocolate Granola

Servings: 1-3
Prep Time : 10 minutes
Cook Time : 20-25 minutes
Total Time : 35-40 minutes

Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper or a silicone baking mat. In a large mixing bowl, combine the rolled oats, chopped nuts, sunflower seeds (if using), cocoa powder, salt, and cinnamon (if using), stirring to evenly distribute the ingredients.

In a separate bowl, whisk together the honey or maple syrup, melted coconut oil, almond butter, and vanilla extract until smooth and well combined. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry mixture and stir until everything is well coated; if it seems too dry, add an extra tablespoon of honey or oil.

Spread the granola mixture evenly on the prepared baking sheet, pressing it down lightly with the back of a spoon to help it clump together. Bake for 20-25 minutes, stirring once or twice to ensure even cooking, and keep an eye on it to prevent burning.

Once golden and crisp, remove from the oven and let it cool completely on the baking sheet. After it cools, stir in the mini chocolate chips, dried fruit, and coconut flakes (if using).

Store the granola in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 2 weeks or in the fridge for up to 1 month. Enjoy as a snack, or serve with milk or yogurt!

Ingredients List

  • Dry Ingredients
    • 3 cups old-fashioned rolled oats
    • 1/2 cup chopped nuts (such as almonds, walnuts, or hazelnuts)
    • 1/4 cup sunflower seeds (optional)
    • 1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
    • 1/4 cup mini chocolate chips (for added chocolatey goodness)
  • VWet Ingredients
    • 1/4 cup honey or maple syrup (for a vegan version)
    • 1/4 cup coconut oil (melted)
    • 1/4 cup almond butter or peanut butter
    • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Seasoning
    • 1/4 tsp salt
    • 1/2 tsp cinnamon (optional, for added warmth)
  • Optional Add-ins
    • 1/4 cup dried fruit (such as cranberries, raisins, or cherries)
    • 1/4 cup coconut flakes (unsweetened, if desired)
