Firecracker Champagne Paloma

Servings: 1
Prep Time : 5 minutes

Rim the glass with salt, if desired, by running a lime wedge around the rim and dipping it in coarse salt.

In a cocktail shaker filled with ice, combine tequila, grapefruit juice, lime juice, and simple syrup. Shake well until chilled, then strain into a salt-rimmed glass filled with ice. Top with Champagne or sparkling wine, and add a dash of chili powder for a fiery twist if desired.

Garnish with a grapefruit slice or lime wheel, and enjoy the Firecracker Champagne Paloma!

Ingredients List

  • Base Spirits
    • 1 oz tequila
  • Citrus
    • 2 oz fresh grapefruit juice
    • ½ oz fresh lime juice
  • Sweetener
    • 1½ oz simple syrup
  • Sparkling
    • Champagne or sparkling wine
  • Spice
    • A dash of chili powder
  • Garnish
    • Grapefruit slice or lime wheel
    • Coarse salt
