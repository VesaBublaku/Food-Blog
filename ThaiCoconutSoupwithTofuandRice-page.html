Thai Coconut Soup with Tofu and Rice

Servings: 4- 9 servings
Prep Time : 10-15 minutes
Cook Time : 25-30 minutes
Total Time : 45-55 minutes

Start by cooking the rice: rinse 1 cup of jasmine rice under cold water and add it to a pot with 2 cups of water and a pinch of salt. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat, cover, and simmer for about 15-20 minutes until the rice is cooked and the water is absorbed.

While the rice cooks, heat 2 tablespoons of coconut oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add 1 small sliced onion and sauté for about 5 minutes until softened, then stir in 2 cloves of minced garlic and 1 tablespoon of grated fresh ginger, cooking for another 1-2 minutes until fragrant.

Add 2-3 smashed stalks of lemongrass and 3-4 torn kaffir lime leaves, cooking for 1 minute to release the flavors. Pour in 4 cups of coconut milk and 3 cups of vegetable broth, followed by 1 tablespoon of soy sauce, 1 tablespoon of fish sauce, 1 tablespoon of brown sugar, and 1-2 bird’s eye chilies (or chili flakes) for heat.

Bring the soup to a simmer and cook for 10-15 minutes to meld the flavors. Add 1/2 cup of cubed tofu, 1 cup of sliced mushrooms, and 1/2 cup of halved cherry tomatoes (if using), and cook for another 5-7 minutes until the tofu is heated through and the mushrooms are tender.

Stir in 1/2 cup of baby spinach, if desired, and cook for another minute until wilted. Taste and adjust the seasoning with salt, pepper, and lime juice to balance the flavors. To serve, ladle the soup into bowls, add a scoop of rice, and garnish with fresh cilantro, sliced red chilies, and toasted coconut flakes if desired.

Ingredients List

  • For the Soup
    • 2 tablespoons coconut oil (or vegetable oil)
    • 1 small onion, thinly sliced
    • 2 cloves garlic, minced
    • 2-3 stalks lemongrass, cut into 2-inch pieces and smashed
    • 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, gratedd
    • 3-4 kaffir lime leaves, torn into pieces
    • 4 cups coconut milk (full-fat for richness)
    • 3 cups vegetable broth (or water)
    • 1 tablespoon soy sauce (or tamari for gluten-free)
    • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
    • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
    • 1-2 Thai bird’s eye chilies
    • 1/2 cup tofu (firm or extra-firm), cubed
    • 1 cup mushrooms, sliced (shiitake, button, or your choice)
    • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved (optional)
    • 1/2 cup baby spinach (optional)
    • Salt and black pepper, to taste
    • Fresh lime juice (about 1-2 limes, adjust to taste)
  • For the Rice
    • 1 cup jasmine rice (or any rice you prefer)
    • 2 cups water (or vegetable broth for extra flavor)
    • Pinch of salt
  • Optional Garnishes
    • Fresh cilantro, chopped
    • Sliced red chili (for extra heat)
    • Sriracha sauce or chili paste for extra spice (optional)
    • Chopped green onions or scallions
    • Toasted coconut flakes (optional)
